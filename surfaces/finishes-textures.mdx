The right surface finish transforms a countertop from a functional surface into a defining design statement. Cambria offers four high-performance finishes — from radiant high-gloss to silky low-sheen — each delivering the same exceptional durability while giving you complete freedom to match your aesthetic. Layer in one of three distinctive textures and you unlock an even greater dimension of tactile and visual character. Whether you're designing a bright, modern kitchen or a moody, intimate bathroom, there's a Cambria finish and texture combination built for your vision.

## Quartz Finishes

Cambria's finishes are specifically engineered for natural quartz surfaces, designed to catch every eye and add beauty across the entire slab. From a radiant high-gloss shine to a subtle, silky sheen, each finish is a distinct design choice — not a performance compromise.

<Note>
  All four Cambria finishes are backed by the **transferable Full Lifetime Warranty**, providing peace of mind for as long as you own your home. Performance is identical across all finishes — the only difference is aesthetic.
</Note>

### Polished

Always radiant under its deserved spotlight, the Polished finish lets every design detail reveal itself in sophisticated style. Its high-gloss surface reflects light beautifully, amplifying color depth and making veining patterns feel alive. Polished is the classic choice for spaces where you want maximum luminosity and drama.

**Best for:** Traditional, transitional, and glam-inspired kitchens and bathrooms where light and sparkle are central to the design.

### Cambria Matte®

Cambria's signature Cambria Matte® finish offers a silky-smooth, low-sheen surface with a touch of class you have to feel to believe. It reduces glare and softens reflections, creating a calm, understated elegance that suits modern and minimalist spaces especially well. The matte surface is just as durable and easy to clean as its polished counterpart — its quieter visual presence is purely a matter of style.

**Best for:** Contemporary, Scandinavian, and nature-inspired interiors where subtle luxury takes center stage.

### Cambria Satin™

The Cambria Satin™ finish features a low-sheen surface with subtle, perceptible texture that adds deeper dimension to the Cambria design beneath it. It occupies the ideal middle ground between the high reflectivity of Polished and the total matte of Cambria Matte® — giving you a finish with gentle warmth and tactile interest that shifts beautifully under changing light conditions.

**Best for:** Transitional and warm-modern spaces where you want visual depth without high shine.

### Cambria Luxe™

Boasting a breathtaking level of brilliance, the Cambria Luxe™ finish redefines elegance with its unparalleled shine and ultra-high-polish surface. Luxe goes beyond standard polished to deliver a mirror-like luminosity that transforms the quartz into something extraordinary. This exclusive finish is currently available only on **Annaleigh™** and **MonTaaj™**.

**Best for:** Statement installations — large kitchen islands, primary bath vanities, and feature walls — where you want an unforgettable, showroom-quality impact.

<Info>
  Cambria Luxe™ is exclusively available on the **Annaleigh™** and **MonTaaj™** designs.
</Info>

## Quartz Textures

Cambria's textured surfaces go beyond finish to add three-dimensional character to your countertop. Each texture enhances both the visual and tactile experience of the surface — ranging from soft veining contrast to more pronounced dimensional detail.

### Inverness®

The Inverness® texture features gently debossed veining that sits against the surface with vibrant intensity, creating a striking contrast between the recessed veins and the field of the slab. This subtle dimensionality brings the design to life in a way that a flat finish alone cannot achieve.

### Satin Ridge™

Satin Ridge™ is a distinctive, sensual texture that adds powerful tactile dimension and visual sophistication to the surface. Run your hand across it and you'll immediately understand the difference — Satin Ridge creates a surface experience that's as memorable to touch as it is to see.

[Explore Satin Ridge designs →](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/satin-ridge-designs)

### Sculpted

Cambria's Sculpted texture adds softly tactile, sculptured veining that contrasts gently against the surface, delivering modern appeal and organic warmth. The effect evokes the hand-worked quality of natural stone while maintaining the consistency and performance of engineered quartz.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Are Cambria finishes resistant to scratches and stains?">
    Yes. All Cambria finishes are designed to be durable and long-lasting, just like the quartz surfaces they sit on. Cambria quartz is nonporous and highly resistant to scratches and stains — regardless of which finish you choose.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there any difference in performance between Cambria's finishes?">
    No. All four finishes provide the same exceptional level of performance. You can select the finish that best suits your style without any compromise on durability, stain resistance, or ease of cleaning.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What should I consider when choosing a countertop finish?">
    Think about three things: your overall aesthetic (how much shine fits the space?), your lifestyle (a matte or satin finish can be more forgiving of everyday smudges in busy kitchens), and the lighting in your room (polished finishes amplify both natural and artificial light). Ultimately, the best finish is the one you love most — and since performance is equal across all options, there's no wrong answer.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get any finish on any design?">
    Not every finish is available on every design. Cambria Luxe™, for example, is exclusive to Annaleigh™ and MonTaaj™. When browsing designs, you can filter by finish to see which options are available for each specific design. Your authorized Cambria dealer can also confirm availability.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I care for different Cambria finishes?">
    All Cambria finishes are cared for the same way — simply wipe with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap if desired. No sealing, polishing, or reconditioning is ever required. For more detail, see the [Product Care guide](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/product-care).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
