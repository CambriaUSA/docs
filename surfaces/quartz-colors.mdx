## Popular Color Categories

### White Quartz

White quartz is one of Cambria's most sought-after categories — and for good reason. A stunning white surface makes any kitchen, bath, or unique application shine in its own light. Whether you prefer a pure, bright white or a softer off-white with subtle veining, Cambria's white quartz collection offers a wide range of designs to elevate the look of any room.

### Black Quartz

Black quartz effortlessly combines breathtaking visual appeal with unparalleled durability. Its deep, rich surface transforms kitchens, bathrooms, and distinctive spaces into bold modern showpieces. Pair black quartz with brass hardware for a luxe contrast, or let it stand alone as a dramatic focal point.

### Gray Quartz

Gray quartz offers the ideal balance of sophistication and practicality. With unique tones ranging from cool silver to warm charcoal, gray quartz provides a neutral anchor that works with virtually any cabinet color, hardware finish, or flooring material — making it a timeless choice for any design vision.

## How to Find Your Perfect Design

Selecting the right Cambria design is a personal process, and the tools below make every step easier.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Explore the full palette online">
    Browse by color, pattern, finish, and thickness at [cambriausa.com](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors) to build a shortlist of designs that match your vision.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order samples">
    Request small select cuts of your favorite designs from [Cambria's online sample shop](https://shop.cambriausa.com/collections/samples) to compare finishes and tones in your own lighting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visualize in your space">
    Use Cambria's Room Visualizer tool to see how your shortlisted designs look in a kitchen or bath setting before committing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View full slabs at a gallery">
    Visit an authorized Cambria Gallery or Showroom to experience full-size slabs in person — the most reliable way to assess the true scale, movement, and depth of any design.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **Samples are small select cuts of a full slab.** Because Cambria designs feature natural variation in veining, pattern movement, and color depth, a sample alone may not fully represent how the design will look at countertop scale. Always view a full slab at an authorized gallery before making your final decision.
</Info>
