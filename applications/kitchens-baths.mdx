## Kitchen Countertops

Cambria quartz kitchen countertops are crafted for durability and style, ready to embrace every moment of daily kitchen life. The surface is scratch resistant and nonporous, so it never absorbs food, liquids, or bacteria — and it never needs sealing, polishing, or reconditioning. To clean, simply wipe with warm water, a soft cloth, and mild soap if desired.

- **NSF-51 food safety certified** — confirmed safe for direct food contact and preparation
- **Scratch and chip resistant** — holds up to knives, cookware, and the demands of a busy kitchen
- **Zero maintenance** — no sealing required, ever; backed by a transferable Full Lifetime Warranty
- **Perfect for islands** — available in 2cm and 3cm thicknesses for a bold, substantial profile; wide slab sizes minimize seams across large islands

<Warning>
  Quartz is not heat-proof. Always use trivets or hot pads under cookware straight from the stove or oven — never place extremely hot objects directly on a quartz surface. Always follow your fabricator's installation guidelines.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  Considering a kitchen island? Cambria's large-format slabs reduce or eliminate visible seams, giving your island a clean, continuous look that highlights the full beauty of the design.
</Tip>

## Shower Walls

Transform your shower into a serene retreat with Cambria quartz walls. Unlike tile, quartz shower walls are nonabsorbent and have no grout lines — eliminating the hiding places where mold, mildew, and bacteria typically grow. The lightweight 1cm thickness is specifically engineered for vertical applications, making installation smooth and efficient.

- **No grout lines** — sleek, continuous surface that's effortless to keep clean
- **Nonabsorbent and nonporous** — naturally resists mold, mildew, and bacteria without any sealant
- **1cm thickness** — lighter weight for faster installation in tight or tall shower spaces
- **Minimal cleaning required** — a soft cloth, warm water, and mild soap are all you need
- Precision-cut to your exact dimensions for a flawless, polished finish

## Planning Your Kitchen or Bath Project

Good planning makes the difference between a surface you like and one you love. Before you visit a Cambria retailer or schedule a consultation, a few simple steps will help you get the most out of your selection process.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Photograph your space">
    Take photos of your kitchen or bathroom from multiple angles, including close-ups of cabinets, flooring, fixtures, and any existing tile or hardware you plan to keep.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bring paint and flooring samples">
    Your countertop or vanity top will live alongside your wall colors and flooring every day. Physical samples help you evaluate undertones and contrast in your actual lighting conditions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Think about surface flow">
    Consider how your surfaces connect — countertop to backsplash, vanity to shower wall. Choosing designs from the same Cambria collection creates a cohesive, intentional look throughout the space.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order quartz samples">
    Cambria offers complimentary samples so you can see and feel the surface in your own home before committing. Order online or visit a retail location near you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

  Ready to start planning? Visit the [Planning Overview](/planning/overview) for guidance on measurements, installation, edge profiles, and working with a Cambria professional.
