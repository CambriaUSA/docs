## Fireplaces

A fireplace is already the heart of a room вЂ” Cambria quartz makes it the centerpiece of your entire home. With effortless elegance, crisp whites, deep dramatic hues, and every design in between, a Cambria quartz surround transforms an ordinary hearth into a work of art that adds warmth and sophistication to any gathering space.

Cambria's lightweight **1cm slabs** are specifically designed for vertical applications like fireplace surrounds. They're quicker to install, easier to handle, and customizable to virtually any shape or size вЂ” so your vision isn't constrained by material limitations.

**Benefits for fireplace surrounds:**
- Scratch and chip resistant вЂ” stays pristine even with frequent use
- Nonporous and stain resistant вЂ” a quick wipe is all it takes to clean
- Zero maintenance вЂ” no sealing, polishing, or reconditioning required
- Available in a wide range of designs, from modern minimalist to richly traditional
- Customizable shapes and sizes with 1cm lightweight quartz

<Warning>
  Cambria quartz is **not heat-proof** and is not rated for direct exposure to open flames. Fireplace surrounds must maintain proper clearance between any active flame or direct heat source and the quartz surface. Always follow both the fireplace manufacturer's guidelines and Cambria's installation guidelines to ensure lasting performance.
</Warning>

## Wall Cladding

Cambria quartz wall cladding transforms ordinary walls into extraordinary focal points that leave a lasting impression. Say goodbye to grout lines, constant upkeep, and uninspired surfaces вЂ” and hello to the art of simplicity, redefined.

Whether you're cladding a dramatic feature wall in a living room, elevating a hotel lobby, or extending a kitchen backsplash to full height, Cambria's **1cm quartz** is the ideal material for vertical applications. It's lighter weight for easier installation, precision-cut to your exact specifications, and available in hundreds of designs.

**Why choose quartz for wall cladding:**
- **No grout lines** вЂ” sleek, seamless surfaces with no joints to clean or re-grout
- **Nonabsorbent** вЂ” naturally resists mold, mildew, and bacteria for a healthier environment
- **Scratch and impact resistant** вЂ” walls stay stunning from day one and every day after
- **Limitless design versatility** вЂ” from bold statement walls to subtle, textural accents
- **1cm thickness** вЂ” specifically designed for vertical applications; lighter and faster to install than 2cm or 3cm slabs

### Bookmatching

**Bookmatching** is a technique where two quartz slabs are mirrored to create a continuous, symmetrical pattern вЂ” like opening a book. The result is a bold, dramatic focal point that commands attention on walls, shower enclosures, fireplace surrounds, backsplashes, and beyond. Cambria's lightweight 1cm quartz makes bookmatching more accessible than ever, delivering effortless installation alongside truly dazzling visual results.
