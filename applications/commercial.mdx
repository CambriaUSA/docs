## Why Cambria for Commercial Projects

### Durability and Design Consistency

Cambria quartz surfaces carry the same performance specifications in a hotel ballroom as they do in a residential kitchen. Scratch resistant, nonporous, and maintenance-free, they hold up to the demands of high-traffic commercial environments without sacrificing the design integrity that defines a premium space. With a palette of hundreds of designs — from understated classics to bold statement surfaces — Cambria gives you the flexibility to create cohesive interiors across every touchpoint of a project.

### Build America, Buy America Act (BABA) Compliant

<Info>
  All Cambria quartz surfaces fully comply with the **Build America, Buy America Act (BABA, Section 70901 of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act)** and the Buy American Act. As an American-made product manufactured in Le Sueur, Minnesota, Cambria eliminates the risk of noncompliance and ensures your projects remain eligible for federal funding — removing a significant procurement risk for publicly funded work.
</Info>

### NSF-51 Food Safety Certified

Cambria quartz is NSF-51 certified for food contact and splash zone safety, making it an ideal surface for commercial kitchens, cafeterias, healthcare break rooms, restaurant counters, and any application where hygiene and food safety standards are non-negotiable. The nonporous, nonabsorbent surface prevents bacterial growth without the need for sealing or chemical treatments.

### GREENGUARD Gold Certified

Cambria quartz meets the rigorous GREENGUARD Gold standard for chemical emissions, satisfying the requirements for **LEED v4 Indoor Environmental Quality** credits. This certification supports your sustainability goals and simplifies the documentation process for green building projects across healthcare, education, corporate, and hospitality sectors.

### Wide Design Selection for Cohesive Interiors

With one of the broadest design palettes in the industry, Cambria gives your project design continuity across multiple surfaces and spaces. Coordinate reception desk cladding with corridor feature walls, match lobby flooring accents with elevator interiors, or carry a single design throughout a hotel's guest rooms and common areas — all from a single manufacturer with consistent quality and availability.

### Healthcare Facilities

Cambria's nonporous, nonabsorbent surface resists the growth of mold, mildew, and bacteria without requiring sealants or harsh chemical treatments. For healthcare environments — from nurses' stations and patient room vanities to break rooms and waiting areas — this translates directly to a cleaner, more hygienic surface that is easier for staff to maintain.

### Corporate Offices

Premium quartz surfaces elevate the design and perceived quality of corporate interiors, from reception desks and conference room tables to pantry countertops and collaborative workspace surfaces. Cambria's durability ensures these surfaces look their best through years of daily use with minimal maintenance.

### Retail and Other Commercial Spaces

Whether you're specifying surfaces for a flagship retail environment, a restaurant, an educational facility, or a government building, Cambria's combination of design versatility, certified performance, and BABA compliance makes it one of the most specification-friendly quartz products available.
