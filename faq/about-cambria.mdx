## About Cambria Quartz Surfaces

<Accordion title="What is Cambria?">
  Cambria is a premium [quartz countertop](/surfaces/quartz-colors) and surfacing product with a wide variety of uses in your home or business — including countertops, backsplashes, floor tile, vanities, fireplace surrounds, wet bars, and showers. It consists of pure, natural quartz — one of the hardest and most common minerals on Earth — combined with pigments and binders.

  Many Cambria designs carry the natural look and feel of granite, marble, and other quarried stones, but with superior performance thanks to quartz's inherent strength and durability. Because Cambria is **nonabsorbent and nonporous**, it will not absorb food or liquids, and it is completely **maintenance free** — simply clean it with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap if desired.
</Accordion>

Use our [retailer locator](https://www.cambriausa.com/dealer-locator/locations) to connect with a dealer near you.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is Cambria a natural stone?">
  Cambria is made from **pure natural quartz**, combined with pigments and binders. Quartz is the extremely hard mineral that gives Cambria its signature strength and durability. While it isn't quarried as a single stone slab, the quartz content is entirely natural — our unique manufacturing process simply transforms it into an elegantly finished surface with superior performance characteristics.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How is Cambria manufactured?">
  Cambria's slab manufacturing facility combines sustainably harvested natural quartz with a small amount of pigment and binders. This proprietary process produces slabs that are **nonabsorbent, durable, and compact** — giving you the beauty of natural stone without the upkeep demands. Every slab is made right here in the United States.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is Cambria seamless?">
  No — Cambria slab sizes differ by design, and the use of seams will vary based on slab size and application. Check with your fabricator to understand what your specific layout will require.

  <Note>
    There may be a visible pattern and shade change at each seam. This is a natural characteristic of working with slab material and does not affect the performance or durability of your surface.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How big is a Cambria slab?">
  Cambria quartz slabs are available in two sizes:

  | Size | Imperial | Metric |
  |---|---|---|
  | Standard Slab | 122″ × 55.5″ | 310cm × 141cm |
  | Jumbo Slab | 132″ × 65.5″ | 335cm × 166cm |

  Slabs are readily available in three thicknesses: **1cm**, **2cm**, and **3cm**. Please confirm the maximum piece size that can be produced from a slab with your fabricator or installer before finalizing your project plan.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Will my slab match my sample exactly?">
  Not exactly — and that's important to understand before installation. The design sample you receive is a small, select cut from a single slab; it does not fully exhibit all the design characteristics of the final installed product. Color movement, veining patterns, and shade variation are all inherent to quartz and do not affect performance.

  <Tip>
    We strongly recommend visiting a local [Cambria gallery or slab showroom](https://www.cambriausa.com/dealer-locator/galleries-showrooms) to view a full-size slab before making your final decision. Seeing the complete slab gives you a much more accurate picture of how your finished surface will look.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>
