Buying Cambria is a straightforward process designed around your project needs. Whether you're working with a kitchen and bath dealer, a builder, or an architect, a network of exclusive Cambria partners is ready to guide you from your first design question to final installation. Here's everything you need to know before you start.

## Getting Started

Follow these two steps to kick off your Cambria project:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a Retailer Near You">
    Use the [Cambria retailer locator](https://www.cambriausa.com/dealer-locator/locations) to find authorized dealers, fabricators, galleries, and slab showrooms in your area. Cambria is available through partner dealers nationwide and at select [The Home Depot](https://cambriausa.com/home-depot) locations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule a Consultation">
    Contact your chosen retailer to schedule a design consultation. To get an accurate estimate, bring your cabinet dimensions, backsplash requirements, edge profile preferences, and the locations and dimensions of your sink and stove. Most showrooms can also help you narrow down your design choices on the spot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Purchasing FAQs

<Accordion title="How can I purchase Cambria?">
  Cambria is available exclusively through **Cambria partner dealers** and at **select The Home Depot locations**. Use the [retailer locator](https://www.cambriausa.com/dealer-locator/locations) to find an authorized partner near you who can walk you through the process, provide a project estimate, and connect you with a certified installer.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Where can I see Cambria on display?">
  Cambria is displayed nationwide at a variety of locations, including:

  - Local and regional **kitchen and bath dealer showrooms**
  - **Fabricator showrooms**
  - **Appliance centers** and **build/design centers**
  - **Model homes**
  - **Cambria galleries and slab showrooms**

  We work exclusively with partners who meet our premium service and installation standards. Use the [dealer locator](https://www.cambriausa.com/dealer-locator/locations) to find a display location near you.

  <Tip>
    Most Cambria galleries and slab showrooms do not require an appointment. However, if there are specific designs you want to see, it's best to call ahead so the showroom team can make sure those designs are available for you.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I work with a builder, architect, or designer?">
  Absolutely. If you're working with a builder, remodeler, architect, interior designer, or kitchen and bath store, simply ask them to specify Cambria for the surfaces you want. If they're not yet familiar with how to source Cambria, they can call toll-free: **1-866-CAMBRIA** (1-866-226-2742). We'll introduce them to the product and connect them with a local fabricator or dealer. You can also point them to the [retailer locator](https://www.cambriausa.com/dealer-locator/locations).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What information do I need before my consultation?">
  To receive an accurate estimate at your consultation, gather the following before you go:

  <CardGroup cols={2}>
    <Card title="Cabinet Layout" icon="ruler">
      Linear dimensions of all cabinet runs that will receive countertop
    </Card>
    <Card title="Backsplash Plans" icon="layers">
      Whether you want a backsplash, its height, and whether it will be Cambria
    </Card>
    <Card title="Edge Profile" icon="pencil-ruler">
      Your preferred edge profile style — your dealer can show you samples
    </Card>
    <Card title="Sink & Stove Info" icon="flame">
      Location and dimensions of your sink, stove, and any other cutouts
    </Card>
  </CardGroup>

  If you have design questions or need help with recommendations, your Cambria partner can provide expert guidance every step of the way.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need an appointment to visit a showroom?">
  Most Cambria galleries and slab showrooms do not require an appointment before visiting. That said, if there are specific designs you're interested in viewing, calling ahead is recommended — it gives the showroom team time to ensure those slabs are accessible and ready for you to see in full scale.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does pricing work?">
  Cambria does not set retail prices. Pricing is established by our trade customers — kitchen and bath dealers, fabricators, and contractors — based on your specific project requirements. Generally, Cambria is **comparable in price to granite and other premium stone surfaces**. Contact a dealer near you for a project-specific estimate.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long does the process take from purchase to installation?">
  The timeline varies depending on your project type, the designs you select, and the schedule of your retailer and installation company. Your retailer or installation company will provide timeline estimates specific to your project after your purchase is confirmed.

  <Info>
    For new construction projects, your builder typically coordinates the installation schedule on your behalf. Be sure to confirm the timeline and responsibilities with your builder in advance.
  </Info>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens after I purchase?">
  Once your purchase is finalized, your installer will work with you to schedule appointments for **measurements and installation**. During the measurement appointment, your installer will template your space to ensure a precise fit before the slabs are cut.

  For new construction, your builder typically coordinates the installation process for you — but it's always a good idea to confirm the details directly with them.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I track my order?">
  Your retailer or installation company is your primary point of contact for order status updates. Reach out to them directly with any questions about scheduling, lead times, or slab availability. If you need further assistance, you can also contact Cambria Customer Care at **1-866-CAMBRIA** (1-866-226-2742).
</Accordion>
