Cambria is a family-owned American manufacturer of premium natural quartz surfaces — countertops, vanities, shower walls, and beyond — engineered for lasting beauty and everyday resilience. Every slab is made in the USA from 93% pure natural quartz combined with pigments and a polymer binder, producing a surface that is harder, denser, and more consistent than most natural stones. Whether you are designing a new kitchen, remodeling a master bath, or adding a dramatic fireplace surround, Cambria gives you the confidence that your surfaces will look extraordinary for the lifetime of your home.

## What Is Cambria Quartz?

Cambria surfaces begin with exceptionally pure natural quartz — one of the hardest minerals on Earth — harvested exclusively from Cambria's own mines. The quartz is combined with carefully selected pigments and a polymer binder to form a slab that is **nonporous**, **nonabsorbent**, and **maintenance free**. Unlike granite or marble, Cambria never needs sealing, polishing, or reconditioning to stay beautiful.

<Info>
  Cambria quartz contains **93% pure natural quartz** by composition. The remaining material consists of pigments for color and a polymer binder that locks everything together into a dense, uniform slab.
</Info>

### Key Performance Attributes

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Nonporous & Nonabsorbent** | Liquids cannot penetrate the surface — no staining from wine, oil, or acids |
| **Stain Resistant** | Resists everyday staining agents without special treatments |
| **Scratch & Chip Resistant** | Mohs hardness of 7/10 stands up to knives, utensils, and daily wear |
| **Maintenance Free** | No sealing, polishing, or reconditioning — ever |
| **NSF-51 Food Safe** | Certified for direct food contact and splash zones |
| **GREENGUARD Gold Certified** | Low VOC emissions for a healthier indoor environment |

### Slab Sizes & Thicknesses

Cambria slabs are available in two formats to suit any project scale, from compact bathroom vanities to sweeping kitchen islands.

| Format | Dimensions |
|---|---|
| **Standard Slab** | 122″ × 55.5″ |
| **Jumbo Slab** | 132″ × 65.5″ |

Slabs are available in three thicknesses to match your design intent and structural requirements:

- **1 cm** — ideal for shower walls, backsplashes, and vertical cladding applications
- **2 cm** — a versatile profile suited for furniture, wall panels, and lighter-duty horizontal surfaces
- **3 cm** — the preferred thickness for kitchen countertops, bathroom vanities, and high-traffic surfaces
