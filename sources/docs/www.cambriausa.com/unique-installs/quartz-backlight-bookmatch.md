## Frequently Asked Questions

---

---

What is quartz backlighting?

Quartz backlighting is a design technique where light panels are placed behind the quartz creating a mildly translucent, glowing surface and appearance. Backlighting is available in select Cambria quartz designs and thicknesses. The effect is best in areas with low to mid-level lighting, where the radiance can truly shine.

Which Cambria designs can be backlit?

Only a select number of Cambria designs are suitable for backlighting, as the process requires a certain level of translucency. Designs that can be backlit include [Brittanicca Warm™](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/brittanicca-warm), [Brittanicca Gold Warm™](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/brittanicca-gold-warm), [Skara Brae®](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/skara-brae), and [Brighton™](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/brighton). It's always best to confirm with your fabricator or Cambria directly to ensure your chosen design is compatible with this technique.

What is quartz bookmatching?

Bookmatching is a technique where two slabs of quartz are cut to create a mirrored, or open book, effect. This creates a continuous, symmetrical pattern that is visually stunning. It’s an excellent way to create a dramatic focal point in any room. Bookmatching is available in select Cambria quartz designs and thicknesses including [MacBeth™](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/macbeth), [Everleigh™](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/everleigh), [Southport™](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/southport), and [Bentley®](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/bentley). See the full list of [available designs here](https://www.cambriausa.com/unique-installs/quartz-backlight-bookmatch#accordion-b457384679-item-e61db4ffc4).

What are the best applications for bookmatching?

Bookmatching is ideal for large, continuous surfaces where the pattern can be fully appreciated. It is often used for feature walls, shower walls, fireplace surrounds, backsplashes, and beyond. The technique adds a bold statement and artistry to wherever it is applied.

Can any Cambria design be bookmatched?

No, not all Cambria designs are suitable for bookmatching. The technique works best with designs that have strong, dynamic veining and movement. Some of the designs that can be bookmatched include [Portrush®](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/portrush), [Annaleigh™](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/annaleigh), [Bentley®](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/bentley), and [Brittanicca](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/brittanicca) [®](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/brittanicca). See the [full list of available designs](https://www.cambriausa.com/unique-installs/quartz-backlight-bookmatch#accordion-b457384679-item-e61db4ffc4).
