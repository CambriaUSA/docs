# 1cm Cambria Quartz Surfaces

---

Take your designs to new heights with thinner 1cm quartz surfaces, perfectly suited for vertical applications and weight-sensitive projects. The possibilities are endless with potential applications including fireplace surrounds, shower walls, backsplashes, wall cladding, and more. Durable and maintenance free, our 1cm option provides the perfect blend of style and practicality.
