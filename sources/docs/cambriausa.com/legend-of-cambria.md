A kingdom torn apart by deception and revenge is reunited by the youngest heir to the throne. Camber battles fate, pure evil, and unexpected forces on his epic journey from boy to king.

# Camber

The king's favorite son to succeed him. As our story unfolds, Camber perfects not only the qualities required to reign, but the skills of a fearless warrior.

A dreamer. A poet. A creative soul. And as the legend unfolds, the king's favorite son to succeed him. From his earliest days, Camber perfects the qualities needed in a leader and regent. And when the moment requires it, the skills of a fearless warrior.

Camber's fall into the water and near-death experience ages him 13 years before he is rescued by Aeres. His life becomes the fulfillment of an ancient prophecy of a king sworn to protect a magical land of unparalleled beauty.

# Aeres

A water nymph charged with protecting the magical waters of Brittanicca, she will be forced to choose between the life of a goddess and a mortal one with Camber.

A water nymph and daughter of the King of the Water Spirits. She is the Lady of the Falls, charged with protecting the magical waters of Brittanicca from intruders. By story's end, she will be forced to choose between the life of a goddess and that of a mortal.

- [Legend Extra]()

### Legend Extra

Fresh-water nymphs inhabited the rivers, lakes, and springs of earth. Like Aeres, they were immortal deities and part of a sacred landscape. Aeres has seen much but experienced little, which leaves her unprepared for her deep feelings for Camber.

# Brutus

Part ruler, part seasoned warrior and all adventurer. Unequaled as regent and protector. Even in his winter years, Brutus is still a force to be reckoned with.

Camber's father is a fair king with the weathered hubris of a seasoned warrior. But even a man of Brutus's experience isn't able to see the decay eating away at his kingdom. He is a respected descendant of the Trojan hero Aeneas, also known in legend as the founder and first king of Galloway.

- [Legend Extra]()

### Legend Extra

Even in his winter years, Brutus still projects an imposing figure. His frame stands straight and true with shoulders wide enough to carry a kingdom. We see in his face the man that Camber will become.

# Queen Aneira

Proud queen and devoted mother. She exudes the strength and stature of a matriarch who's not afraid to bravely wield a sword should the need arise.

Proud queen and devoted mother to Camber, Albanactus, and Locrinus. Her long auburn hair shows the first whispers of silver, which only amplifies her quiet grace and intelligence. She exudes the strength and stature of a matriarch who's not afraid to bravely wield a sword should the need arise.

- [Legend Extra]()

### Legend Extra

Queen Aneira, in death as in life, is the driving force that brings the kingdom together. Her elegant features are visible in all her children. In her passing, she created something that would live forever.

# Albanactus

Albanactus was born with impressive strength and courage, traits that serve him well in battle and in the search for new land.

Albanactus has always exuded the confidence that comes from being born with remarkable ability. An avid hunter and skilled with the bow and arrow, he is the kind of first born any regal father would be proud to hand the kingdom. Fate, however, may have other plans.

- [Legend Extra]()

### Legend Extra

Black-haired and handsome, Camber's eldest brother is a virile young man of 23 when our story begins. Exhibiting a fundamentally likeable and trustworthy air, he patiently awaits his chance at the throne.

# Locrinus

With his remarkable abilities, Locrinus was the kind of first born any regal father would be proud to hand the throne. Fate, however, may have other plans.

The second son, Locrinus, is 21 when we first meet him, but already displays the impressive strength and courage of his father. Competitive to a fault, he's long harbored feelings of jealousy when it comes to his older brother's direct line to the throne.

- [Legend Extra]()

### Legend Extra

A reliable and devoted brother, Locrinus is designated to be a trusted advisor to the heir of the throne, his elder brother, Albanactus. And while he doesn't always understand Camber's ways, he tries to be a responsible sibling.

# Ruger

The scarred and brutal visage of a hardened warrior and merciless killer. His humiliation at the hands of Camber sets him on a dangerous course of revenge.

The scarred and brutal visage of a hardened warrior and merciless killer, Ruger's relentless need to conquer doesn't grow from a desire to rule, but simply to satisfy his unquenchable thirst for battle and delight in the suffering of others.

- [Legend Extra]()

### Legend Extra

Ruger had suffered a wrong at the hands of the King's father, and for that reason, more willingly cast his lot with evil exiles. His humiliation at the hands of Camber sets him on a lifelong course of revenge.

# Bloudewedde

Ruger's conniving sidekick, spy and traitor to the royal family. It's his breach of trust that opens the door to Galloway's swift downfall.

Ruger's conniving sidekick, and a close confidant to the royal family. As he quietly turns traitor, it's his breach of trust that leaves the kingdom gates open for the march of evil. The first step in the swift downfall of Galloway.

- [Legend Extra]()

### Legend Extra

He is the well-educated master of a dozen languages and a trusted confidant of the royal family, but as fate would have it, he becomes their biggest enemy.

# Kelpie

The Kelpie are the supernatural shape-shifting water horses that roam the land, rivers, and streams of Brittanicca.

The Kelpie are the supernatural water horses that roam the land, rivers, and streams of Brittanicca. Feared by some as guardians of the land, they also act as messengers and guides to those strong of character. It's a Kelpie who ultimately assists Camber on his journey to find safe haven.

### Legend Extra

The mythical guardian of the land, its most common guise is that of a beautiful horse standing by the riverside—a tempting but deadly ride for a weary traveler.

# Ddraig Goch

Intelligent, aggressive and a formidable foe, it's Ddraig Goch who provides a blazing defense of an overmatched Camber.

Entombed by rocks and held in a deep slumber for thousands of years, Ddraig Goch is the legendary dragon who is freed by Camber. It's then the dragon's turn to deliver the ultimate defense of Camber in order to repay him for his good deed.

- [Legend Extra]()

### Legend Extra

Ddraig Goch is known for his intelligence and aggressive nature. His serpentine form slithers and snakes its way through the sky with grace and speed while the large, membranous wings stretch between his limbs to support flight.

# Rock Giants

Rock giants embody a vaguely human shape, comprised of large boulders and stones. Like a constant avalanche passing on level ground, they are incredibly powerful.

Huge and imposing, the Rock Giants are brutish beasts summoned from the Earth to protect a captured Kelpie. The only thing that can stop a Rock Giant is water, knowledge Camber puts to good use.

- [Legend Extra]()

### Legend Extra

Rock giants are comprised of large boulders and stones over a molten lava core that enables their movement. Like a constant avalanche passing on level ground, they are incredibly powerful.

# Galloway

Galloway is a land of contrasts—steep slopes of bracken and heather stud a coastline carved by centuries of beating waves.

Our tale begins here with Brutus and Aneira on the throne, under siege from the barbarian King Ruger. Galloway is a land of contrasts—steep slopes of bracken and heather, long white beaches, and forested tunnels of oak leading to the barren rock that studs the coastline, carved by centuries of beating waves.

# Brittanicca

Untamed beauty, shaped by flat coastal plains that give way to breathtaking mountains. Brittanicca is a land still haunted by legend.

Untamed beauty, shaped by flat coastal plains that give way to barren hills that stretch skyward into dramatic cliffs that tumble into the ocean. Brittanicca—discovered before the twilight that gave birth to the Druids, Kelpie, and Land Giants—is a land still haunted by legend. Human life here is unsettled at best, as our itinerant travelers are soon to discover.

# Cambria

Sweeping vistas eventually resolve in the grandeur of the coastline. The perfect setting for the final clash between good and evil.

It is here that the final clash between good and evil occurs and, from that crucible, a kingdom reigns. In Cambria, gentle paths wind beneath low cliffs, punctuated by flower-speckled meadows. Sweeping vistas are accented by dramatic waterfalls before resolving in the grandeur of the coastline. A perfect home for the new kingdom.
