## What Is a Countertop Edge Profile?

---

An edge profile is the finishing touch that completes your [countertop](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/kitchen-countertops) look, so it’s wise to put some thought into your decision. Need help narrowing down your choices? We’ve handpicked an array of Cambria edge profiles so you can customize your countertops to suit your style.

---

## Finding Your Cambria Edge Profile

---

With a variety of Cambria edge profiles, there’s an option for every aesthetic from contemporary to traditional. Because Cambria’s quartz designs infuse veining, movement, and tones throughout the slab, the beauty of your design will carry through any edge profile you choose.

---

### Rounded Style Countertop Edges

---

Fully and partially rounded countertop edges add subtle softness to a space. The smooth detailing can make for a comfortable dining area at an island or add contrast to angular fixtures.

---

**Edge: Piedmont**

The Piedmont countertop edge profile is softly rounded on the top edge. You may also hear it referred to as a Demi Bullnose, ½ Bullnose, or a ¼ Round. This edge is available in 2cm, 3cm, and 4cm thickness.

### Profiles with Rugged Texture

---

A hand-chiseled countertop edge adds dynamic texture and a rustic look. The edge is not polished and feels rugged to the touch.

---

![Moraine quartz countertop edge from Cambria ](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/cambriacompanyllc/moraine-chiseled?qlt=85&ts=1698420914025&dpr=off)

![a simple illustration of a Moraine edge profile from Cambria](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/cambriacompanyllc/edge-profile-graphics-edge-only-moraine?qlt=85&ts=1698420914343&dpr=off)

**Edge: Moraine**

This Cambria quartz countertop edge profile is hand-finished for a natural, rustic appearance. You may hear the Moraine edge profile called a Chiseled Edge, Broken Edge, or Rock Face. This edge is available in 2cm, 3cm, 4cm, and 6cm thickness.

### Profiles That Make a Bold Statement

---

A mitered edge gives the appearance of a thicker countertop for a crisp, clean edge. This is a stunning option to showcase the bold veining of designs like [Brittanicca](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/brittanicca).

---

![Rimrock quartz countertop edge from Cambria ](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/cambriacompanyllc/rimrock-miter?qlt=85&ts=1698420913974&dpr=off)

![a simple illustration of a Rimrock edge profile from Cambria](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/cambriacompanyllc/edge-profile-graphics-edge-only-rimrock?qlt=85&ts=1698420914259&dpr=off)

**Edge: Rimrock**

The Rimrock countertop edge profile, also known as a mitered edge, is assembled from two pieces. This adds a weighty appearance to the design without requiring a heavier or thicker countertop. Cambria’s proprietary techniques combine the two pieces for a cohesive look. This edge is made from 1cm, 2cm, and 3cm stone that is mitered to the desired height for the thicker edge aesthetic

### Countertop Edges with Intriguing Depth

---

Add depth and dimension to your countertop with angled or beveled edges.

---

![Ledge quartz countertop edge from Cambria ](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/cambriacompanyllc/ledge-md?qlt=85&ts=1698420914154&dpr=off)

**Edge: Ledge**

The Ledge countertop edge profile fades back at the bottom edge for a versatile look with subtle yet captivating dimension. Sometimes called a Reverse Bevel or Plater, this edge is available in 3cm thickness.
