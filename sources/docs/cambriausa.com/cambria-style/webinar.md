## _Cambria Style_ Design Event

---

In this illuminating conversation, Cambria’s Head of Design Summer Kath asks interior designers Christopher Kennedy and Heather and Matt French how they design spaces that reflect what their clients love and how they live. From a modern retreat with spa-like amenities to a full renovation layered with rich colors and floral patterns, you’ll see how well-considered design styles don’t copy trends but tell a uniquely personal story.
