## The Characteristics of Quartz

---

---

Cambria quartz countertops give you incredible maintenance-free performance, superior durability, and a variety of unique colors and designs you’ll love.

[Learn more about Cambria quartz countertops](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/why-cambria)

---

### 

Colorful Creations

Using pure, white quartz, we are able to create stunning countertops featuring a wide array of designs, colors, and patterns. Beyond countertops, quartz can be used in unique applications like [shower walls](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/shower-walls), [backsplashes](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/kitchen-backsplashes), and [fireplace surrounds](https://cambriausa.com/unique-installs/quartz-fireplaces).

### 

Durability

Quartz countertops are extremely durable and withstand daily use with ease for life. As a hard material, quartz resists chips, scratches, and stains. Cambria quartz surfaces are backed by a transferable Full Lifetime Warranty to provide peace of mind for a lifetime.

### 

Maintenance Free

Quartz never has to be sealed or [maintained](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/product-care). Extremely durable and nonabsorbent, it is easy to clean, is stain resistant, and doesn’t harbor harmful bacteria. Stone countertops, including quartz, can be damaged by sudden temperature changes. Place pot holders or closed trivets under anything hot.

### 

Environmentally Friendly

Quartz is harvested in small aggregate stone form which leaves a smaller footprint and obtains better yield per harvest, generating less waste. The native vegetation restoration more optimally occurs after the quartz is harvested in this manner.

## Frequently Asked Questions

---

---

Do Cambria quartz countertops require care and maintenance?

No, keeping with its [care-free promise](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/product-care), Cambria requires no sealing, polishing, or reconditioning. Simply wipe with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap, if desired. Due to its nonabsorbent nature, it does NOT require regular sealing and is stain resistant.

How can I purchase Cambria quartz countertops?

Purchasing Cambria is easy. Use our [retailer locator](https://cambriausa.com/dealer-locator/locations) to find a retail location near you that can provide you with an estimate for your countertop needs. Generally, you will need the dimensions of your cabinet layout, information regarding backsplash requirements, edge profile selections, and your sink and stove locations and dimensions to get an accurate estimate.  If you have design questions or need help with recommendations, each of our partners can provide their expertise to step you through.

How much do Cambria quartz surfaces cost?

Generally, Cambria prices are comparable to granite and other stone surfaces. Our trade customers such as kitchen and bath dealers, fabricators, and contractors establish the [price of Cambria quartz surfaces](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/how-much-do-quartz-countertops-cost) for homeowners.
