- **Internet or other electronic network activity information** (for example, browsing history, interactions with our Website or Apps)

### D.  Your Rights Under the CCPA/CPRA

As a California resident, you have the following rights:

- **Right to Know:** Request the categories and/or specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you in the past 12 months.
- **Right to Delete:** Request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions (for example, legal obligations or fraud prevention).
- **Right to Correct:** Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
- **Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing:** Request that we do not sell or share your personal information, where applicable.
- **Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information:** If we collect sensitive personal information, you can limit its use to only necessary purposes.
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** You will not be discriminated against for exercising your rights under the CCPA/CPRA.

You may submit a privacy rights request by contacting us at:

- [CambriaPrivacy@CambriaUSA.com](mailto:cambriaprivacy@cambriausa.com)
- Or via phone number listed on our Website

You may only make a verifiable consumer request for access or data portability **twice within a 12-month period**.

### A. Your Data Protection Rights Under the GDPR

If you are located in the European Union, United Kingdom, Switzerland, or another jurisdiction that provides similar rights, you have the following rights under the GDPR, subject to certain exceptions:

- **Right of access:** You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to rectification:** You have the right to request that we correct or complete your personal data if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
- **Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”):** You can request the deletion of your personal data where legally applicable.
- **Right to restrict processing:** You can request that we restrict the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
- **Right to data portability:** You may request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format and, where possible, that it be transmitted to another controller.
- **Right to object:** You can object to the processing of your personal data based on our legitimate interests, including for direct marketing purposes.
- **Right to withdraw consent:** If processing is based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [CambriaPrivacy@CambriaUSA.com](mailto:cambriaprivacy@cambriausa.com). We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Notice or as required by law. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time.

Please note that User Contributions you submit may be publicly accessible, and we may not be able to delete or retrieve this data once it enters the public domain or is redistributed by third parties.

### B. Legal Bases for Processing

We rely on the following legal bases under the GDPR to process your personal data:

- **Consent:** Where you have given us explicit permission to process your data for specific purposes.
- **Contract:** Where processing is necessary to enter into or perform a contract with you.
- **Legal obligation:** Where we are legally required to process your data.
- **Legitimate interests:** Where processing is necessary for our legitimate interests, such as improving our services, communicating with users, and securing our systems, provided those interests are not overridden by your data protection rights.

If we rely on consent as a legal basis, you may withdraw it at any time by contacting us at [CambriaPrivacy@CambriaUSA.com](mailto:cambriaprivacy@cambriausa.com). If we continue to process your data based on another legal ground, we will inform you accordingly.
