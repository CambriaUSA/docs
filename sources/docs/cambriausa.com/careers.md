## Career Opportunities at Cambria

---

### 

Manufacturing

At Cambria's state-of-the-art, nearly 1 million square foot, slab manufacturing facility in Minnesota, we relentlessly pursue excellence in production, research, and development, engineering, quality, safety, maintenance, and automation.

![](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/cambriacompanyllc/hr-plant-soft-line-040-23?qlt=85&ts=1726599466953&dpr=off)

### 

Sales and Marketing

Our impressive quartz countertops deserve marketing that stands out and a sales approach that’s above all others. We do this with stories that connect, images that inspire, and relationships that care. From our internal marketing team to our sales representatives throughout the world, we work together to make a beautiful difference.

![](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/cambriacompanyllc/sales-outdoors-034-23?qlt=85&ts=1726599475225&dpr=off)

### 

Customer Care

Our customers’ loyalty and happiness is our true measure of success. From our call center, online, and in-person, our Customer Care representatives assure our customers are heard, answered, and respected from first introduction to install.

![](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/cambriacompanyllc/cambria-employee-branding-ep-011-23%3A2-up-Images-644x550?ts=1726599482862&dpr=off)

Information Technology

Information drives innovation. By monitoring and maintaining our computer systems and networks, our IT departments enable our work and advance our capabilities.

Human Resources

## Benefits at a Glance

---

Working for Cambria gives you access to a world-class benefits package. As our employee, you’ll enjoy Health and Dental Insurance, Paid Time Off, 401(k) plus matching, Discretionary Profit Sharing, Flexible Spending Account or Health Savings Account, Referral Program, Tuition Reimbursement, Employee Assistance Program, Employee Discount, Professional Development Assistance, and Life, Supplemental Life, and Disability Insurances.
