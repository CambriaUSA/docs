## Living Our Values

---

The only thing stronger than the quartz surfaces we create is staying true to our values. Placing ethics at the heart of our business is a powerful compass. It means, together, we’re expanding opportunities through education, fostering inclusivity, and making a lasting difference for people, our business partners, and communities.
