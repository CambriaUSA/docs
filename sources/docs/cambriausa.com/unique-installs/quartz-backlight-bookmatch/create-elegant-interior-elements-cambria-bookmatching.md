Create Interior Elements with Bookmatching

Bookmatch technology produces a mirroring effect that fuses both art and design for an impactful visual statement. Using this technique expands your design opportunities in both residential and commercial applications.

## Dramatize the Natural Aesthetics of Quartz

Bookmatching creates a mirroring effect of a Cambria quartz slab like the pages of an open book. It’s a perfect way to showcase the natural beauty of Cambria, transforming it into a breathtaking, statement-making piece of art. For example, at the four-star [Omni Viking Lakes Hotel](https://cambriausa.com/professionals/case-studies/omni-viking-lakes-hotel), the design team used [Brittanicca Gold Warm™](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/brittanicca-gold-warm) in an en suite bathroom, bookmatching to align the design’s veining in such a way as to add a really beautiful backdrop to a freestanding soaking tub.

In another project, an office breakroom incorporates an oversize [Bentley®](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/bentley) island with bookmatching detail to highlight the impressive scale of the artful piece. The elegant beauty this technology brings to the island means the space needs little else in terms of distinctive features to be a showstopper.
