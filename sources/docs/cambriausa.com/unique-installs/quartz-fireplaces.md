## Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cambria?

Cambria is a [quartz countertop](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops) and surfacing product that has a variety of uses in your home or business such as [countertops](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/kitchen-countertops), [backsplashes](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/kitchen-backsplashes), floor tile, [vanities](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/bathroom-vanities), [fireplace surrounds](https://cambriausa.com/unique-installs/quartz-fireplaces), wet bars, [showers](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/shower-walls), etc. Cambria consists of pure, natural quartz—one of the hardest and most common minerals on Earth—and pigments and binders. Some of our designs have the natural look and feel of [granite](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-vs-granite), [marble](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-vs-marble), and [other quarried stones](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops#comparisons), but they all feature superior performance because of Cambria’s strength and durability. Cambria is nonabsorbent and nonporous and will not absorb food and liquids. And best of all, it is maintenance free. Quartz just needs to be cleaned with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap, if desired. Learn more about the [benefits of Cambria](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/why-cambria).

Can Cambria quartz be used for fireplace surrounds?

Yes, Cambria quartz can be used for fireplace surrounds. Our durability, scratch resistance, low maintenance, and the wide range of design options make it a popular material for this application. However, it is critical that the installers and specifiers strictly adhere to both the fireplace manufacturer’s and Cambria’s installation guidelines for Cambria to succeed in the application. Use our [retail locator](https://cambriausa.com/dealer-locator/locations) to find a professional partner near you.

What are the benefits of a Cambria quartz fireplace surround?

Cambria’s natural quartz surfaces bring calm sophistication to any fireplace surround design. Our lighter-weight [1 cm slabs](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/thicknesses-edge-profiles/1cm) are ideal for vertical applications like fireplace surrounds—quicker to install, nonabsorbent, resistant to scratches and stains, maintenance free with simple cleaning and no sealing or polishing, shape and size customization options, and available in a palette of styles from light to dark to suit any space. Learn more about the [benefits of Cambriaopens in a new tab](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/why-cambria).

When deciding between quartz and other surface options, price often plays a big role. From material costs to installation and upkeep, you’ll want to [consider all factors before you make your investmentopens in a new tab](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/how-much-do-quartz-countertops-cost). Generally, Cambria prices are comparable to other stone surfaces like granite. Our professional partners such as kitchen and bath retailers, fabricators, and contractors establish the price of the Cambria quartz surfaces for homeowners.

How do I clean and maintain a quartz fireplace surround?

Cambria quartz requires no sealing, polishing, or reconditioning. Simply wipe with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap, if desired. Due to its nonabsorbent nature, it does NOT require regular sealing and is stain resistant.
