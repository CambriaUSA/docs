Thin 1cm Quartz Slabs for Wall Cladding

Our [1 cm quartz slabs](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/thicknesses-edge-profiles/1cm) are reshaping how to think about vertical surfaces. Wall cladding adds architectural interest to a space. And our 1cm quartz slabs provide durability and design versatility in one thin surface. Custom‑fabricated for your interiors, this lighter-weight material makes installation easier while its scratch resistance means you can have millwork that’s as practical as it is beautiful.

### Elegance That Lasts

The nonporous nature of Cambria quartz slabs means they won’t harbor bacteria, mold, or mildew—making Cambria a smart choice for areas prone to moisture, like bathrooms and kitchens. There’s no grout to scrub or seal. Just a soft cotton cloth, warm water, and a mild soap, if desired, is enough to keep your quartz surfaces looking like new.
