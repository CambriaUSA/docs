## Frequently Asked Questions

---

---

Can Cambria quartz be used for furniture?

Yes, you can use Cambria quartz for a variety of custom furniture, from dining or coffee tables to desks and credenzas. Its durability, scratch resistance, and low maintenance make it ideal for high-traffic spaces. Cambria quartz can be custom crafted into virtually any shape or size, giving you the ability to create unique, one-of-a-kind furniture pieces. You can see quartz furniture creations in our [Inspiration Galleryopens in a new tab](https://cambriausa.com/inspiration-gallery-residential?applicationType=furniture&applicationType=coffee%20table&applicationType=credenza&applicationType=desk&applicationType=reception%20desk&applicationType=side%20table&applicationType=tabletop). How much does Cambria quartz custom furniture cost?

When deciding between quartz and other surface options, price often plays a big role. From material costs to custom needs and intricacies, you’ll want to [consider all factors before you make your investment.opens in a new tab](https://www.cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/how-much-do-quartz-countertops-cost) Our professional partners such as kitchen and bath retailers, fabricators, and contractors establish the price of the Cambria quartz surfaces for homeowners.

What are the benefits of building furniture with Cambria quartz?

Cambria quartz is nonabsorbent, resistant to scratches and stains, and maintenance free with no sealing or polishing required, making it ideal for high traffic use. Plus, it can be crafted into almost any shape or size across a wide range of colors, patterns and designs to create one-of-a-kind furniture. [Learn more about the benefits of Cambriaopens in a new tab](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/why-cambria).

Is Cambria quartz furniture durable enough for everyday use?

Yes, Cambria quartz is extremely durable, making it perfect for furniture that sees high traffic and daily use. It resists scratches and stains without any sealing or polishing. 

How do I clean and maintain quartz furniture?

Cambria quartz requires no sealing, polishing, or reconditioning. Simply wipe with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap, if desired. Due to its nonabsorbent nature, it does NOT require regular sealing and is stain resistant.
