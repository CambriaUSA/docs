### Subtle Contrast

Light color palettes need a little contrast to create visual interest. Here, [Berwyn®](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/berwyn) adds an appearance of texture in color tones that complement the beachy blue-gray theme of this space. This quartz design is also a terrific alternative to granite because its natural swirls of color and stone veining are consistent which allows you to better match your design preferences.

---

## Sleek and Stylish

The wide variety of color and pattern options in our design palette allows you to create virtually any look in your space. For lovers of a more modern aesthetic, [Carrick™](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/carrick) design adds the bold look of concrete in a quartz surface that can be fabricated for specific customization. Here, the combination of this sleek fireplace focal point with a more rustic wood ceiling treatment and large windows blurs the line between indoor and outdoor beauty. And gray quartz designs pair well with most any color scheme, making a gray-toned quartz fireplace surround a lasting element in your home that can work well with any future updates in your furnishings or décor.

[Brittanicca®](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/brittanicca) and [Brittanicca Gold Warm™](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/brittanicca-gold-warm) draw the eye along with the designs’ organic movement. A simple shift in the direction of the surface’s veining from horizontal to vertical can accomplish different design goals. Whether it’s balancing vibrant artwork above a mantel or drawing your vision upward and becoming the art itself, the gentle movement in these classic quartz designs create perfect focal points as fireplace surrounds.
