## Frequently Asked Questions

---

---

Can quartz be used for walls?

Yes, Cambria quartz wall cladding can be used in multiple wall applications including feature walls, full height backsplashes, shower walls, and fireplace surrounds. We recommend quartz wall cladding be installed by a professional from our network of fabricators and installers in order to ensure it is installed correctly. Quartz wall cladding is typically created from [1cm](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/thicknesses-edge-profiles/1cm) or [2cm](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/thicknesses-edge-profiles) quartz slabs.

How much does a quartz wall cost?

When deciding between quartz and other surface options, price often plays a big role. From material costs to installation and upkeep, you’ll want to [consider all factors before you make your investment](https://www.cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/how-much-do-quartz-countertops-cost). Our professional partners such as kitchen and bath retailers, fabricators, and contractors establish the price of the Cambria quartz surfaces for homeowners.

What are the benefits of using quartz for wall cladding?

Quartz wall cladding offers numerous advantages over traditional materials like tile or paint. It is incredibly durable, resistant to scratches and stains, and easy to clean. Because it is nonporous, it won’t harbor mold, mildew, or bacteria, making it a hygienic choice for any room. Cambria quartz surfaces offer an extensive palette of designs from light to dark to suit any space and can be customized to almost any shape or size. 
 
Cambria 1 cm quartz surfaces are especially ideal for vertical applications like wall cladding, since they are lighter weight and easier to handle and install. Learn more about the [benefits of Cambria](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/why-cambria).

Does quartz wall cladding have grout lines?

No, one of the biggest advantages of Cambria quartz wall cladding is the absence of grout lines. This creates a sleek, continuous look that is both modern and easy to maintain. Without grout, you won’t have to worry about scrubbing away dirt and mildew.

How do I install quartz wall cladding?

While there is a time and a place for DIY, installing your quartz wall cladding is not one of them. That’s why we recommend professionals from our network of fabricators and installers do it for you. These professionals can often complete a fast and efficient installation, possibly in a single day. Learn more about the [installation processopens in a new tab](https://www.cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/planning/quartz-installation) or use our [retail locatoropens in a new tab](https://www.cambriausa.com/dealer-locator/locations) to find a professional near you.
