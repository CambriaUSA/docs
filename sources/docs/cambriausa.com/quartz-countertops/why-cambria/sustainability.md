## Caring for People and the Planet

---

Cambria ensures a safe, high-quality product for your home by adhering to industry best practices. This dedication to responsible sourcing and manufacturing results in a smaller environmental footprint and a natural quartz surface that supports a healthy living space for your family.

---

### 

Sustainable Sourcing

Our quartz is responsibly sourced directly from our owned mines. We use a high-yield, efficient extraction process that is designed to minimize waste. This commitment allows us to strictly prioritize sustainability throughout our sourcing operations.

![](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/cambriacompanyllc/mine-canon-drone-007-23%3ALarge-1x1?ts=1776176052859&fit=constrain,1&dpr=off)

---

### 

Mindful Manufacturing

Cambria contributes to a healthier home environment through nonabsorbent, maintenance-free design and manufacturing that is NSF-51 certified for food safety and never needs sealing. Committed to your family’s well-being, our American-made surfaces hold GREENGUARD Gold certification for low VOC emissions, actively contributing to LEED v4 Indoor Environmental Quality standards.

[Learn more about American-made natural quartz surfaces](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/why-cambria/american-made)

![](https://cambriausa.scene7.com/is/image/cambriacompanyllc/cambria-diff-performance-perfected-inspection%3ALarge-1x1?ts=1776110864167&fit=constrain,1&dpr=off)

---

### 

Thoughtful Transparency

Our Health Product Declaration (HPD) v2.1 listing supports complete transparency for projects centered on sustainability, wellness, and environmental responsibility. We are Living Building Challenge (LBC) compliant, featuring a Declare label that verifies our products contain no Red List or EPA/COC/REACH chemicals. Additionally, our mindful MATERIALS listing ensures we are included in the library for accessible product transparency and sustainability information.

[See our safety resources and data sheets](https://www.cambriausa.com/professionals/safety/silica-safety#resources)

![](https://cambriausa.scene7.com/is/image/cambriacompanyllc/cambria-diff-performance-perfected-quartz-003?qlt=85&ts=1776176118860&fit=constrain,1&dpr=off)

---

### 

Responsible Waste Reduction

Sustainability is integrated into every stage, from using renewable energy at our primary mine to optimizing transportation routes for lower emissions. Within our slab manufacturing and fabrication facilities, we prioritize water conservation by recycling one billion gallons annually. Finally, we ensure longevity and reuse at our primary fabrication facility by refurbishing diamond-metal tools.

![](https://cambriausa.scene7.com/is/image/cambriacompanyllc/cambria-diff-foam-cutting-hand-bridge-tile-24-213%3ALarge-1x1?ts=1776111648877&fit=constrain,1&dpr=off)
