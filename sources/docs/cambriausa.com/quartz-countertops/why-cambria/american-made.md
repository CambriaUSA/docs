## 

Built to a Higher Standard

At Cambria, American made is more than a label—it’s a promise to do things the right way. It’s a deep sense of pride in the work we do and the people we do it for.

It means a commitment to higher standards, better craftsmanship, and a responsibility to our communities. We stay true to our roots—designing, producing, and perfecting every slab here in the USA.

From slab production to countertop installation, every Cambria purchase invests over 95% of costs back into the U.S. economy.
