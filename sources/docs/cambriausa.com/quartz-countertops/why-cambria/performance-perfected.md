## Beauty That Doesn’t Back Down

---

### 

Superior Durability 

Scratch, chip, and stain resistance makes Cambria built for real-life demands.

### 

Maintenance Free and Easy Care

No sealing or polishing. Just clean with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap if needed. 

### 

Hygienic and Food Safe

NSF-51 certified for safe food preparation, every Cambria surface is nonabsorbent and meets the highest standards for cleanliness and performance.

Environmental Impact
