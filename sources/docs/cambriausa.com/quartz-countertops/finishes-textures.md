## Quartz Finishes & Textures FAQs

---

---

Are Cambria finishes resistant to scratches and stains?

Yes! Our finishes are designed to be durable and long lasting, just like our quartz surfaces which are nonporous and highly resistant to scratches and stains.

Is there any difference in performance between Cambria's finish options?

All of our finishes provide the same high level of performance. This means you can select the finish that best suits your style without compromising on performance.

What's the difference between Cambria's finish options?

Cambria offers [four high-performance finishes](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/finishes-textures) with varying degrees of sheen. 
[Polished](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/finishes-textures/polished): A radiant high gloss that reflects light beautifully. 
[Cambria Matte®](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/finishes-textures/matte): A silky smooth, low sheen finish with reduced glare. 
[Cambria Satin™](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/finishes-textures/satin): A low sheen finish with subtle texture that adds depth 
[Cambria Luxe™](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/finishes-textures/luxe): Unparalleled shine and high-polish finish redefine elegance.

What should I consider when choosing a countertop finish?

Choosing a countertop finish depends on your style and how you use the space. Consider the overall aesthetic, desired sheen level (how much shine you want), and your lifestyle. Ultimately, the best finish is the one that you love the most!
