## Benefits of White Quartz Countertops

---

The allure of white quartz countertops is nothing short of legendary. From quartz’s modern aesthetic to its easy maintenance, this timeless material is a beautiful addition in homes and commercial spaces alike.

Infusing a contemporary touch into any space, white quartz countertops are the essence of modern sophistication. Whether it’s a sleek kitchen island or a minimalist bathroom vanity, white quartz countertops bring a sense of clean, cutting-edge style that offers effortless care. Unlike porous natural stones, white quartz resists stains and is [simple to clean](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/how-to-clean-quartz-countertops). A soft cotton cloth, warm water, and mild soap, if desired, is all it takes to keep a quartz surface looking pristine, making it perfect for high-traffic areas.

Easy maintenance isn’t the only beautiful benefit. [White quartz](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/cambria-quartz-white-countertop-designs) has a wonderful ability to make spaces feel larger and brighter, and can reflect light around a room—creating an open, airy atmosphere in any room.

The timeless allure of white quartz has captivated designers and homeowners alike, including George Markoutsas of [Ablaze Design Groupopens in a new tab](https://ablazedesigngroup.com/about-us/) (formerly The ABL Group). “From the beginning, we knew we had to have a high-quality, maintenance-free countertop option with the natural look and feel of classic marble. We settled on [Cambria’s Colton® design](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/take-a-tour-of-the-lake-forest-showhouse-featuring-cambria) for its timeless elegance and its warm and creamy tones, which paired nicely with the existing floor.”

White quartz is not confined to a single design style. It effortlessly adapts to [various aesthetics](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/how-to-pair-wood-cabinets-with-white-cambria-countertops), allowing you to create a cohesive look in your home. Whether your taste is [traditional](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/traditional-white-and-warm-kitchens), contemporary, or eclectic, white quartz provides a harmonious backdrop for your décor choices.

There’s no denying that white quartz creates a luminous focal point in any room—be it countertops, accent walls, fireplace surrounds, or even a stunning [white quartz dining table](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/custom-quartz-dining-room-table). This radiant centerpiece is exactly what’s needed to tie an entire space together.

Explore all the ways white quartz can make your [kitchen, bathroom,](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths) and [shower walls](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/shower-walls) stand out. Or, to get inspired by some of our best white quartz countertops, [read our blog](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog).

Marble-Look White Quartz

There are many reasons to choose white quartz over marble. White quartz has a striking [resemblance to marble](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/marble-designs) but offers incredible durability. Quartz is stain resistant, nonabsorbent, and requires minimal upkeep. While marble and granite may require periodic sealing and polishing, quartz shines with a simple cleaning using a soft cotton cloth, warm water, and mild soap, if desired. Even more alluring is its ability to be used in unique applications—unlocking incredible creative possibilities.

## White Quartz Countertops: What to Consider

---

White quartz is stunning in its own right. But there’s more to know beyond its exceptional performance and immaculate beauty. Here are our top things to consider before you invest in a white quartz countertop by Cambria—and why it might make for a wonderful addition to your home.

Quartz is durable and nonabsorbent, which means it’s easy to clean and resists stains and harmful bacteria. It also never has to be sealed or maintained. When it comes time to clean it, just wipe it down with a soft cotton cloth, warm water, and mild soap, if desired.

While incredibly durable, all natural stone countertops can be damaged by sudden temperature changes. Therefore, the use of a closed-weave hot pad or solid trivet such as a breadboard is always recommended to prevent heating the product. But you should never worry about daily use. As an extremely hard material, quartz is scratch resistant. And while that’s good for your countertops, that’s bad for your knives. If you cut directly on your countertop, it could dull your knives. It’s best to always use a cutting board. To learn more about how to care for quartz, [read our FAQs](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/faq/product-care).

Solid white quartz countertops not your thing? It doesn’t have to be. At Cambria, we use pure, white quartz before transforming it into a wide array of designs, colors, and patterns—including a [dazzling display of veining](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/explore-these-6-new-veined-white-quartz-countertop-designs-from-cambria).

Find a Cambria [retailer near you](https://cambriausa.com/dealer-locator) to discuss your project and get a quote.

Are white Cambria quartz countertops resistant to surface damage?

White quartz countertops from Cambria are durable and more resistant to surface damage than other stone. However, all stone can be damaged by force and no stone is chip- or scratch-proof. Objects hitting edges, particularly at sinks or dishwashers, may cause chips. Surface markings are more visible on monotone designs than multi colored surfaces.

Do white Cambria quartz countertops stain?

No matter the color—white, [black](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/black-designs), or [gray](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/gray-designs)—the nonporous and nonabsorbent surface of Cambria’s white quartz countertops are resistant to staining and eliminates the need for any sealing. Its nonabsorbent surface also greatly reduces the potential for bacterial growth from food absorbing into your countertop.
