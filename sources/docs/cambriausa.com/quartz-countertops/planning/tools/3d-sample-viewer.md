## Frequently Asked Questions 

---

---

What is the Cambria 3D Sample Viewer and how does it help me?

Our new 3D Sample Viewer is an interactive tool that lets you explore Cambria designs in three dimensions, right from your web browser.

- See every angle, detail, and feature up close
- Get a realistic sense of the design
- Make a more informed and confident decision before ordering a sample or visiting a showroom

Is it easy to use? What are the controls?

Yes, it’s designed to be simple and intuitive. 
**On Desktop:**

- **Rotate:** Click and hold your **left mouse button**, then move your mouse OR **click the arrows** in the on-screen Rotate controls.
- **Zoom:** Use your **mouse’s scroll wheel** or use the **magnifying glass buttons and slider** in the on-screen Zoom controls.
- **On a Phone/Tablet:** Use one finger to **swipe to rotate** and two fingers to **pinch to zoom**.
- **Change Finish:** If more than one finish is available, **click the icon** next to the selected finish to open the menu and choose another to view.

Do I need to install any special software or apps?

No. There are no plugins to download or apps to install. It works seamlessly on modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. To ensure you can view our 3D Sample Viewer's models, your web browser needs to support WebGL, which is standard on most modern browsers. If you encounter problems, updating your browser and graphics drivers, or enabling WebGL in your browser's settings, will usually resolve the issue.

Will this work on my smartphone or tablet?

Absolutely. The viewer is fully responsive and optimized for touch devices. You can explore our entire 3D catalog from your desktop computer or on your phone while on the go.

How accurate are the 3D models to the real-life product?

Please note that actual colors, textures, and design features will vary from the images in this tool. To see the true beauty and detail in Cambria designs, we recommend visiting a showroom to see a full slab, viewing design specific product pages, or ordering Cambria quartz samples prior to purchasing.

The viewer is running slowly or isn’t loading. What should I do?

While our 3D Sample Viewer is highly optimized for performance, rendering complex, high-resolution graphics in real time can be demanding. The initial loading time may vary based on your device’s processing power and internet speed. For optimal performance, we recommend using a modern browser on a desktop or laptop computer. If you experience issues, please try these quick fixes:

1. Refresh the Cambria web page.
2. Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
3. Make sure your web browser is updated to the latest version.
