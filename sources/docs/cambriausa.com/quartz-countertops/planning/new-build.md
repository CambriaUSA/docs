## Planning Your New Build

---

---

Building a new home is a big undertaking. But when it comes to adding Cambria, all it takes is a few simple steps to be well prepared. 

### 

Pick Your Partner

The beauty of a new build is you don’t have to design it yourself. Trust your builder and professional designers to inspire you and inform you along the way. Professionals will help keep your project on time and on [budget](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/how-much-do-quartz-countertops-cost). That’s why you hired them.

### 

See Your Future

Visualize your new home. Who is there? How do you feel? Where do you spend your time? What do you love about it? Think through these details. There’s no need to rush when building your dream home.

### 

Create Your Space

A countertop is more than a surface. Carefully consider how you’ll use it. Be selective and confident with your choice so you’ll love it for life. [Discover the Cambria Difference](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/why-cambria) and see why quartz outshines all other options.

### Explore Cambria Designs

When dreaming, dream big. You’re creating your future home after all. Look online and in magazines for inspiring paint colors, flooring, cabinets, and accents. Or visit our design detail pages to see expert-curated moodboards that highlight the paint colors, fixtures, and accents that go best with each of our quartz designs.

## The Installation Process

---

Your Cambria quartz surface will be installed by professionals from our network of fabricators and installers, ensuring that every detail is expertly handled while making your dream space a reality. Learn more about the [installation process](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/planning/quartz-installation) and be prepared for every step along the way.

---

### Measurement

An official measurement of your space will be performed by your professional partner. Once completed, they should be in touch to let you know next steps.

## Take Our Advice

---

---

We know what it takes to have your Cambria install go smoothly in your new build. Follow a few tips to ensure yours does as well.

### 

Start Inspired

Share inspiring photos or samples of items you’ve purchased or desired with your designer or Cambria Gallery or Showroom consultant. Or visit the design details page to see which paints, faucets, and cabinets our experts recommend pairing with specific Cambria designs.

### 

Follow the Flow

Your kitchen countertops can influence what you do throughout your home. Start there before moving to backsplash tiles, cabinets, shower walls, or fireplace surrounds. Add hardware and lighting before moving on to paint. Want advice? Your builder and designer always have great ideas.

### 

Prepare for Install

Since countertops are placed on top of existing structures, all cabinets, appliances, and other structures must be installed first before your Cambria countertop installation can happen.
