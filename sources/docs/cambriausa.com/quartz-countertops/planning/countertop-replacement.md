## Expert Tips for a Smooth Project

---

Ensure your project goes well by following these expert-approved suggestions, designed to help you properly prepare for your countertop replacement.

### Existing Countertops

Have a plan for the removal of your existing countertops. Feel free to ask your Cambria installer if they can provide this service.

### 

Sink and Faucet

If you’ve decided to purchase a new sink or faucet, please have these items on hand during your countertop measure appointment.

### 

Installation Day

Declutter your space prior to installation day. Clear all walkways and remove all items from counters, floors, and connecting cabinets.
