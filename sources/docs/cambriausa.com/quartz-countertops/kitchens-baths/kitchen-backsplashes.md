---

With beauty that tile simply can’t match, our quartz delivers effortless elegance and durability that’s also maintenance free and nonabsorbent to resists stains and bacteria, so your spaces stay as clean as they are stunning.

---

### 

Beautifully Hardworking

Kitchens are the center of everyday activity, but that doesn’t mean your backsplash shouldn’t stay flawless. Cambria natural quartz surfaces are built to withstand daily wear, including resisting chips and scratches, so your space looks flawless no matter what you have cooking.

### 

Carefree Cleanliness

No sealing, no stress—just a smooth, nonabsorbent surface that wipes clean in an instant. Spills and splashes don’t stand a chance, so you can spend more time enjoying your kitchen and less time maintaining it.

### 

Unlimited Style

With a wide range of colors, patterns, and designs, Cambria helps create a kitchen that feels uniquely yours. Plus, without grout lines, your backsplash effortlessly flows from countertop to wall, delivering a striking, uninterrupted look.

Tailored to You

Every kitchen is different, and your backsplash should be, too. Our lightweight 1cm quartz surfaces are a smart, custom-crafted solution designed to fit your space with a blend of beauty and practicality.

## Frequently Asked Questions

---

---

How much does a quartz kitchen backsplash cost?

When deciding between quartz and other surface options, price often plays a big role. From material costs to installation and upkeep, [you’ll want to consider all factors before you make your investment](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/how-much-do-quartz-countertops-cost). Our professional partners such as kitchen and bath retailers, fabricators, and contractors establish the price of the Cambria quartz surfaces for homeowners.

How thick is a quartz kitchen backsplash?

A quartz backsplash is most commonly available in two thicknesses: 1 cm or 2cm. Both work well for this type of application and come with multiple benefits over traditional tile solutions. Learn more about Cambria [1 cm slabs](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/thicknesses-edge-profiles/1cm) or [2 cm slabs.](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/thicknesses-edge-profiles)

How do I choose a kitchen backsplash with quartz countertops?

The benefit of quartz countertops can extend to a matching or coordinating quartz kitchen backsplash. When choosing a backsplash to go with your quartz countertops, it's good to first consider the overall style of your kitchen and the color and pattern of your countertops. With the broad palette of Cambria designs and colors, you have endless options from creating a fully cohesive look to a strong contrasting look. Use our [retail locator](https://cambriausa.com/dealer-locator/locations) to find a professional to see samples in real life and discuss design options.

Is quartz a good a choice for a kitchen backsplash?

Yes, quartz is a great option for your kitchen backsplash. Without grout lines, your backsplash can flow from countertop to wall, providing an uninterrupted look. Quartz is nonabsorbent, stain resistant, and maintenance-free, protecting your space from water, grease and everyday kitchen messes. Additionally, its durability can withstand the daily wear and tear of a busy kitchen.

How do I install a quartz kitchen backsplash?

While there is a time and a place for DIY, installing your quartz backsplash is not one of them. That’s why we recommend professionals from our network of fabricators and installers do it for you. These professionals can often complete a fast and efficient installation, especially compared to a tile backsplash. To ensure a smooth installation day, they need to measure your space and account for all custom areas. Learn more about the [installation process](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/planning/quartz-installation) or use our [retail locator](https://cambriausa.com/dealer-locator/locations) to find a professional near you.

How do I clean and maintain a quartz kitchen backsplash?

Cambria quartz requires no sealing, polishing, or reconditioning. Simply wipe with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap, if desired. Due to its nonabsorbent nature, it does NOT require regular sealing and is stain resistant.
