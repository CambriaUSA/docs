## The Benefits of Natural Quartz Bathroom Vanities

---

A Cambria quartz vanity isn’t just a pretty surface—it’s a powerhouse of style and function. Naturally nonabsorbent, it stands up to moisture, resists mold and bacteria, and makes cleaning effortless—so no scrubbing is required. More time to carry on, beautiful.

---

### 

Designed to Stay Clean

Bathrooms are full of moisture, but Cambria won’t show it. Our nonporous quartz surfaces keep stains and bacteria from taking hold, so your vanity stays fresh and flawless.

### 

No Upkeep

Splashes, spills, and everyday messes? Cambria quartz surfaces handle it all without the need for sealing. Just a quick wipe with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap, if desired, and voilà, you’re done.

### 

Everyday Tough

From morning routines to bedtime wind-down, bathroom vanities get a lot of use. Our ultra-durable natural quartz surface resists scratches and stains, showing up on the daily without showing wear.

### 

Style That Speaks

From modern elegance to timeless charm, Cambria has a design to match. With a wide range of colors and patterns, vanity tops can be as bold or as subtle as desired.

## Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cambria?

Cambria is a [quartz countertop](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops) and surfacing product that has a variety of uses in your home or business such as [countertops](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/kitchen-countertops), [backsplashes](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/kitchen-backsplashes), floor tile, [vanities](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/bathroom-vanities), [fireplace surrounds](https://cambriausa.com/unique-installs/quartz-fireplaces), wet bars, [showers](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/shower-walls), etc. Cambria consists of pure, natural quartz—one of the hardest and most common minerals on Earth—and pigments and binders. Some of our designs have the natural look and feel of [granite](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-vs-granite), [marble](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-vs-marble), and [other quarried stones](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops#comparisons), but they all feature superior performance because of Cambria’s strength and durability. Cambria is nonabsorbent and nonporous and will not absorb food and liquids. And best of all, it is maintenance free. Quartz just needs to be cleaned with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap, if desired. Learn more about the [benefits of Cambria](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/why-cambria).

Can I use quartz for a bathroom vanity top?

Yes, quartz is an excellent choice for a bathroom. Cambria quartz is naturally nonabsorbent and so resists mold, mildew, and bacteria–with no sealing, reconditioning, or polishing required. It is also highly durable, resisting scratches and stains, perfect for a high-traffic bathroom. It is easy to clean with just a quick wipe with warm water, a soft cotton cloth and mild soap, if desired.

When deciding between quartz and other surface options, price often plays a big role. From material costs to installation and upkeep, [you’ll want to consider all factors before you make your investment](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/how-much-do-quartz-countertops-cost). With our extensive design palette, you can find a color or style that best suits your needs. Learn more about [replacing your countertop](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/planning/countertop-replacement).
