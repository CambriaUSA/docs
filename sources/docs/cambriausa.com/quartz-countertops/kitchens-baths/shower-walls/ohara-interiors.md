Material Serenity

When it came to transforming her personal bathroom into a spa-like oasis, Kate O’Hara, CEO and Creative Director for O’Hara Interiors, sought a restorative sense of calm that was achieved through materiality, including Cambria quartz surfaces throughout.

![](https://cambriausa.scene7.com/is/image/cambriacompanyllc/everleigh-warm-bathroom-stephanie-penick-kate-ohara-cs30-002-26?qlt=85&ts=1778267150192&fit=constrain,1&dpr=off)

Everleigh Warm™

## Create the Perfect Look with Varied Cambria Quartz Thicknesses

Ultra-thin, lighter-weight 1cm quartz slabs streamline installation for quicker project completion. Combine with thicker countertop surfaces for a varied visual aesthetic in your space. O’Hara says, “I loved installing the Cambria vertically in the shower. The design pattern gives the shower a sculptural feeling.”

### Why Choose Cambria?

“When I think of luxury, I think of spaces that support you, where you can walk in and your body relaxes,” says O’Hara. “I wanted an elevated material that could also perform. I wanted the surfaces to stay crisp and beautiful without feeling fragile.”

---

## Project Goals

Clean lines and strong materials, with a lot of visual quiet. With Cambria quartz surfaces, a bathroom can feel warm and spa-like without leaning on textiles or busy finishes.

### Choosing a Design

“Everleigh Warm™ hit the exact tone I wanted,” says O’Hara. Bringing the quartz up the wall behind the vanity, as well as behind the tub, ties the room together and also protects against splashes and spills. “This renovation improved my life more than I expected. It feels good every day.”
