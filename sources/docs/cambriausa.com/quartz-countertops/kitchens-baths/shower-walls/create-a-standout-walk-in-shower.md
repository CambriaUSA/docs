## All the Way Up

Beyond bathroom vanity tops, our quartz surfaces can be installed on shower walls, shower curbs, shower casings and jambs, as well as on overhead applications. This stunning shower is clad with Annicca in a space large enough for bench seating and several showerheads. Each slab was inspected, photographed, and added to a special system so the pieces could be arranged and rearranged to create the most aesthetically pleasing layout. Our quartz slabs are produced large enough to accommodate these types of applications which makes for a more pleasing-to-the-eye and high-performing result.

## Beyond the Bathroom

Cambria was installed throughout this light and airy lakefront home. Taking full advantage of the vertical application possibilities, [Summerhill®](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/summerhill) wraps an oversized [fireplace feature](https://cambriausa.com/unique-installs/quartz-fireplaces). The shades of white with large veins of silver and black in this quartz design sparkle and shimmer in the reflection of the lake from the room’s picture window. Summerhill also tops the kitchen island located opposite the sitting area in this open-concept space. It’s a perfect complement to the white cabinetry and the warm wood millwork on the ceiling. A wet bar in an ancillary space has added dimension with a layer of our Harlech™ design rich in copper and gold hues that ground the lake blue tile backsplash.
