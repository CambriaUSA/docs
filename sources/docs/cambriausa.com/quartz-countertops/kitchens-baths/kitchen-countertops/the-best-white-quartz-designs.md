## Create a Light, Bright Focal Point

[White quartz countertops](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/white-designs) are the perfect complement to any design aesthetic from traditional to modern. And since today’s kitchens are essentially the hub of the home where many day-to-day activities happen, like cooking, you can maintain a clean and uncluttered look in your space with white-toned countertops. This is especially true for those set on incorporating more intrepid elements in their kitchen design like industrial metal fixtures, dramatic flooring, or darker stained woods. White quartz countertops ensure your space will still feel open and airy.

And if you prefer a mostly all-white kitchen, the variety of Cambria white quartz design options provides opportunity to introduce a bit of drama without overpowering the more uniform aesthetic you’re trying to achieve. In addition, this versatility means you can select a white quartz design that complements and never competes with the other white tones throughout your space.
