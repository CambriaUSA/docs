## Easily Update Your Space

A countertop refresh is the simplest way to update and elevate your kitchen without renovating your entire space. By simply [updating your countertops](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/planning/countertop-replacement), you can eliminate extensive planning and up to 70 to 90 percent of the costs of a full renovation. Since countertops are often the focal point of any dream kitchen, modernizing this one element is so visually impactful, it’s sure to help you better enjoy cooking in your beautifully updated space. It’s also a quick and easy way to boost the value of your home since a [Cambria quartz countertop](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops) delivers much more than beauty. All our quartz surfaces are designed with extreme durability to withstand daily wear and tear. They are nonabsorbent, stain resistant, and easy to clean. Plus, they are backed by a transferable [Full Lifetime Warranty](https://cambriausa.com/warranty), giving you peace of mind along with added home value.

Installing new quartz countertops also saves time. A typical kitchen remodel can leave you without a kitchen for up to two months or more. Cambria is professionally installed in three easy steps: a measurement appointment, countertop customization, and installation day.
