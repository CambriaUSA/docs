### Livable Luxury

The stunning simplicity of [Cambria Black™](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/cambria-black) countertops anchors this otherwise eclectic kitchen design. Every space within this home was designed to evoke high-end luxury while maintaining durability and functionality, giving a nod to the home’s modernist roots.

## Artistic by Design

Inspired by the owner’s extensive art collection, the design elements within this home create one-of-a-kind, personalized spaces. Each selected Cambria design fills a distinct artistic purpose while accommodating the homeowner’s desire for low-maintenance luxury.
