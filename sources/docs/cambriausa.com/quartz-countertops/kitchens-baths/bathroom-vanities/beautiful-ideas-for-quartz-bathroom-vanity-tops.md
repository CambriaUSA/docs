### Create Classic Elegance

The warm neutrals and intriguing movement of [Highgate™](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/highgate) pair well with traditional white or gray cabinetry. This combination supports glamorous elements like polished chrome faucets and fixtures. Select a statement mirror and décor to complete a picture-perfect bathroom vanity.

---

## Contrast and Coordinate

Add some luxe to farmhouse stylings by pairing rustic dark woods and shiplap with the refined look of quartz countertops and more contemporary round mirrors. The marble-like look of our [Torquay®](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/torquay) design on this countertop provides just enough contrast for proportionality in a delightfully homespun interior concept. Additionally, pairing your bathroom vanity countertops with your shower surfaces, like with our [Whitney®](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/whitney) design shown below, creates a cohesive look. Cambria quartz surfaces are ideal for [full shower walls](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/shower-walls), shower benches, tub surrounds, and many other applications because they are [easy to clean](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/how-to-clean-quartz-countertops) and hygienic—no moisture or bacteria can seep through this nonabsorbent material.

## Contemporary Spin

Playing with varying shapes and textures is a great way to enliven the neutral palette of more contemporary spaces. Pair gray-toned woods with [Swanbridge™](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/swanbridge) on a vanity countertop for added versatility. The delicate marbled background of this quartz design softens the angular lines of the cabinetry, hexagon mirror, and square floor tiles.

Another marble-like quartz design, [Brittanicca®](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/brittanicca), serves a similar purpose—adding gentle movement and a sense of light and airiness when paired with contemporary hardware and fixtures in [matte black](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/matte-black-kitchen-dining-room-bathroom) or brushed brass. For more dramatic impact, a design like [Rose Bay™](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/designs/rose-bay) creates a distinctively sophisticated aesthetic. Lastly, quartz bathroom vanity tops showcase a spa-like sense of luxury in your bathroom. You can take this vibe a step further with an even more striking floating quartz vanity. A drop-mitered edge on a floating vanity transforms your bathroom countertop into a bold centerpiece along with a sleek and unique storage solution.
