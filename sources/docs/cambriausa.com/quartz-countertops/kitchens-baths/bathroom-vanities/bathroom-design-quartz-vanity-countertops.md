## Integrated Everything

[Quartz fabrication](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/how-to-add-impact-and-personality-to-your-kitchen-design) can go a step further to include integrated features like sink and storage. Beyond the beauty of this smooth modern look is that quartz can withstand daily use in high-traffic areas like bathrooms. Unlike other materials, Cambria is extremely durable, is nonabsorbent, and resists chips, scratches, and stains.
