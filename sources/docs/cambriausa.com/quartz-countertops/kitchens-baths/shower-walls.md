## Frequently Asked Questions

---

---

How thick should quartz shower walls be?

A quartz shower wall is most commonly available in two thicknesses: 1 cm or 2cm. Both work well for this type of application and come with multiple benefits over traditional tile solutions. Cambria 1 cm slabs provide a lighter-weight option that can mean quicker installation and easier handling even in tight or high spaces. Learn more about Cambria [1 cm slabs](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/thicknesses-edge-profiles/1cm) or [2 cm slabs](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/thicknesses-edge-profiles).

Is quartz a good choice for shower walls?

Yes, quartz is an excellent choice for shower walls due to its non-absorbent and durable properties. Because it is non-porous, it resists moisture, mold, and bacteria growth, and there are no grout lines to clean or maintain. You can enjoy simple cleaning with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap if desired–no sealing or polishing is ever required. This makes for a hygienic and maintenance-free option for a wet environment like a shower.

Where and how do I buy a Cambria quartz shower wall?

Cambria quartz is only available through Cambria partner retailers. Use our [retail locator](https://cambriausa.com/dealer-locator/locations) to find a location near you that can provide an estimate and plan for your shower wall needs.

How do I install a quartz shower wall?

While there is a time and a place for DIY, installing your quartz shower wall is not one of them. That’s why we recommend professionals from our network of fabricators and installers do it for you. These professionals can often complete a fast and efficient installation, especially compared to a tile shower wall. To ensure a smooth installation day, they need to measure your space and account for all custom areas. Learn more about the [installation process](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/planning/quartz-installation) or use our [retail locator](https://cambriausa.com/dealer-locator/locations) to find a professional near you.

How do I clean and maintain quartz shower walls?

Cambria quartz requires no sealing, polishing, or reconditioning. Simply wipe with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap, if desired. Due to its nonabsorbent nature, it does NOT require regular sealing and is stain resistant.
