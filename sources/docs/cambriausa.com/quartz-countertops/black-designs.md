## Benefits of Black Quartz Countertops

---

With deep, dark designs, black quartz countertops are boldly captivating. From their modern look to their practical, maintenance-free nature, these sleek surfaces make an irresistible and enduring addition to any home or business.

Black quartz countertops add a touch of class and sophistication to any space. Whether it’s a daring [matte black kitchen](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/matte-black-kitchen-dining-room-bathroom) island or a statement-making bathroom vanity, black quartz brings refinement and a luxurious style that effortlessly complements your décor. 

Its opaque beauty easily creates a striking focal point that draws all eyes and leaves a lasting impression—whether it’s used as countertops, [accent walls](https://cambriausa.com/unique-installs/quartz-wall-cladding), a [fireplace surround](https://cambriausa.com/unique-installs/quartz-fireplaces), or even a bold [dining table](https://cambriausa.com/unique-installs/quartz-furniture). Nothing commands attention quite like black quartz with its incredible depth and richness. 

Easily able to capture a wide range of design aesthetics, [black quartz](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/black-quartz-countertops-versatility) makes the perfect solution for those looking to create a cohesive look throughout a home. Whether your style is traditional, contemporary, or eclectic, black quartz provides a backdrop like no other.

What people love about popular soapstone, you’ll enjoy even more with black quartz. As an [alternative to soapstone](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/soapstone-designs), black quartz provides a similar look with easy upkeep.

Naturally incredibly durable, resistant to stains, and [easy to clean](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/how-to-clean-quartz-countertops), black quartz is the ideal choice for high-traffic areas. It even retains its lustrous black finish without requiring special care or maintenance. 

The timeless allure of black quartz has captivated designers and homeowners alike, providing a canvas for creativity. From modern kitchens to opulent [bathroom vanities,](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/bathroom-vanities) black quartz countertops add an air of sophistication and luxury to any space, and they serve as a versatile centerpiece that ties together the entire room.

Look to black quartz to elevate any space with elegance and drama. Or see how a [black matte surface](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/cambria-blackpool-matte-soapstone-alternative) easily absorbs light while ironically being the highlight of the room. Explore all the ways black quartz can make your kitchen, bathroom, and various other surfaces truly stand out, or gain inspiration from our collection of stunning black quartz designs.

## 

Soapstone-Look Black Quartz

If you’re deciding between soapstone and black quartz, it’s central to consider performance. Soapstone countertops are composed of talc, one of the softest materials on Earth. Quartz, however, is one of the hardest minerals on Earth, making black quartz incredibly durable, resistant to stains, and easy to clean and maintain. The ideal choice for high-traffic areas, black quartz surfaces also retain their lustrous finish without any special upkeep, proving that black quartz is the perfect alternative to soapstone.

## Black Quartz Countertops: What to Consider

---

When it comes to black quartz countertops, it’s essential to explore their unique characteristics, benefits, and considerations. Here’s what you should know before bringing Cambria black quartz surfaces into your home.

Black quartz countertops offer unmatched elegance. The deep, rich color adds a touch of sophistication, making a bold statement in your kitchen or bathroom.

Just like white quartz, black quartz surfaces exhibit the exquisite appearance of natural stone but without the associated maintenance. They do not need sealing or regular polishing. Black quartz surfaces do tend to show fingerprints more than lighter colors, so light cleaning is recommended to maintain their pristine appearance. Thankfully, cleaning is a breeze. A simple wipe with warm water and a soft cotton cloth keeps them looking their best. 

As one of the hardest minerals on Earth, quartz stands strong against daily wear and tear, resisting stains and scratches. Like all natural stone, however, it’s recommended you use a closed-weave hot pad or solid trivet such as a breadboard under hot pots and pans to protect your countertops from potential heat damage and maintain their sleek appearance for years to come. 

It’s important to think about your design aesthetic as well. A black countertop is intentionally bold by design, so you’ll want to be sure it fits with your personal style and preference.

Whether you prefer the drama of solid black or the elegance of veined patterns, black quartz provides a versatile canvas for your luxurious design ideas. Available in a wide range of design styles, from modern to classic, you’ll be able to create a striking visual impact in any setting.

Want to create an even more refined look? Look to a matte finish. Black [Cambria Matte®](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/finishes-textures/matte) or [Cambria Satin™](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/finishes-textures/satin) countertops can elevate your kitchen, dining room, or bathroom designs.

## Black Quartz Countertops FAQs

---

---

Are black Cambria quartz countertops resistant to surface damage?

Cambria is durable and more resistant to surface damage than other stone. However, all stone can be damaged by force and no stone is chip- or stain-proof. Objects hitting edges, particularly at sinks or dishwashers, may cause chips. Surface markings are more visible on monotone designs than multi colored surfaces.

What are black Cambria countertops made of?

Cambria surfaces are made of exceptionally pure quartz, binder, and pigment. Quartz is one of the most common minerals on Earth. It is also one of the strongest materials with a Mohs hardness scale score of 7 (diamonds score a 10). It is because of Cambria quartz beauty, durability and ease of maintenance that the cost tends to fall on the higher end of the [price range](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/how-much-do-quartz-countertops-cost) for quartz countertops.

Do black Cambria quartz countertops require care and maintenance?

No, keeping with its [care-free promise,](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/product-care) Cambria requires no sealing, polishing, or reconditioning. Simply wipe with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap, if desired. Due to its nonabsorbent nature, it does NOT require regular sealing and is stain resistant.
