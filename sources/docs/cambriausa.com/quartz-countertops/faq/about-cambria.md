## About Cambria Quartz Surfaces

---

---

What is Cambria?

Cambria is a [quartz countertop](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops) and surfacing product that has a variety of uses in your home or business such as [countertops](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/kitchen-countertops), [backsplashes](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/kitchen-backsplashes), floor tile, [vanities](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/bathroom-vanities), [fireplace surrounds](https://cambriausa.com/unique-installs/quartz-fireplaces), wet bars, [showers](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/kitchens-baths/shower-walls), etc. Cambria consists of pure, natural quartz—one of the hardest and most common minerals on Earth—and pigments and binders. Some of our designs have the natural look and feel of [granite](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-vs-granite), [marble](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-vs-marble), and [other quarried stones](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops#comparisons), but they all feature superior performance because of Cambria’s strength and durability. Cambria is nonabsorbent and nonporous and will not absorb food and liquids. And best of all, it is maintenance free. Quartz just needs to be cleaned with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap, if desired. Learn more about the [benefits of Cambria](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/why-cambria).

How does Cambria’s price compare to other natural stone or solid surface competitors?

Generally, Cambria prices are comparable to granite and other stone surfaces. Our trade customers such as kitchen and bath dealers, fabricators, and contractors establish [Cambria retail prices.](https://cambriausa.com/cambria-style/blog/how-much-do-quartz-countertops-cost)

Where can I see Cambria on display?

Cambria is available nationwide at local/regional kitchen and bath dealer showrooms, fabricator showrooms, appliance centers, build/design centers, and model homes—as well as our very own Cambria galleries and slab showrooms. We work only with exclusive partners who meet premium service and installation standards. Check out our [dealer locator](https://cambriausa.com/dealer-locator/locations) for a location near you.

How big is a Cambria quartz slab?

Cambria quartz slabs come in two sizes:

- Standard Slab: 122″ x 55.5″ (310cm x 141cm)
- Jumbo Slab: 132″ x 65.5″ (335cm x 166cm)

Cambria quartz slabs come readily available in three thicknesses:

- [1cm](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/1cm-designs)
- [2cm](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/2cm-designs)
- [3cm](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors/3cm-designs)

Please confirm the maximum piece size that can be produced from a slab with your fabricator or installer.

How do Cambria quartz surfaces compare to granite?

Cambria is made from natural quartz, one of the hardest minerals on Earth, for superior durability and performance. With Cambria, you get the look and feel of granite, but with the benefit of it being nonabsorbent and maintenance free. Simply clean Cambria with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap, if desired. Cambria is stain resistant and never needs sealing or reconditioning. 

How is Cambria different from other quartz surfaces?

We are an [American company](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/why-cambria) in the quartz surfaces business. Our design options are significantly different, reflecting our knowledge and responsiveness to the marketplace. Additionally, we are a family-owned company with more than [60 years of manufacturing experience](https://cambriausa.com/about-us).

How many designs is Cambria available in?

Cambria is available in an expansive design palette. [View our collection](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/quartz-colors) to see the variety of our designs.

Is Cambria quartz surface a natural stone?

Cambria is made from pure natural quartz, pigments, and binders. Quartz is the extremely hard mineral that gives Cambria its strength. Our unique process results in a quartz product of elegance, style, and superior durability.

Is Cambria quartz made or manufactured in a plant?

Our slab manufacturing facility converts natural quartz into slabs by combining quartz with a small amount of pigment and binders. Through the process, we are able to create slabs that are nonabsorbent, durable, and compact.

Is Cambria seamless?

Cambria slab sizes differ based on the design; the use of seams will vary based on slab size and application. Check with your fabricator to understand what your application will require. Note: there may be a visible pattern and shade change at each seam.

What range of Cambria products are available?

Cambria is an excellent surface choice for a wide variety of uses, including kitchen countertops, bathroom vanities, shower and tub surrounds, furniture tabletops and accent pieces, fireplace surrounds, desktops, wet bar areas, and window sills. Cambria surfaces are available in 1cm, 2cm, and 3cm thicknesses. A select number of designs are also offered in 1cm thickness for custom order, with minimal lead times. These thinner slabs are ideal for weight-sensitive or vertical applications such as shower walls, fireplaces, wall cladding, bookmatching, backlighting, and thermoforming. [Various edge profiles](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/thicknesses-edge-profiles) can also create the appearance of a thicker slab. We invite you to explore the [many ways in which Cambria can be used](https://cambriausa.com/inspiration-gallery-residential) throughout a variety of interiors.
