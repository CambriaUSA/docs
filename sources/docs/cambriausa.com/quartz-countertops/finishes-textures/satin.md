## Benefits of Satin Finish

---

Ideal for those seeking a textured, understated look, Cambria Satin’s soft touch enhances ambience and reduces glare to bring elegance and depth to any space.

---

### 

Maintenance Free

Unlike other honed surface finishes, Cambria Satin finish doesn’t require sealing or polishing. Just clean with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap, if desired.

### 

Durable

Cambria Satin finish offers the same optimal scratch and chip resistance as our polished finish, protecting your peace of mind against anything life throws your way.

### 

Luxurious

Cambria Satin’s subtle texture, reduced glare, and added visual depth transform each design into a refined statement.

### 

Guaranteed

Cambria Satin finish possesses the same industry-leading, transferable [Full Lifetime Warranty](https://www.cambriausa.com/warranty) as its polished finish counterpart.
