## Benefits of Luxe

---

Cambria Luxe redefines brilliance, intensifying each surface with an unparalleled high-polish shine that captivates and makes every detail unforgettable.

---

### 

Maintenance Free

Built with easy in mind, Cambria quartz countertops never need sealing, polishing, or reconditioning. Just clean with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap, if desired.

### 

Durable

Cambria Luxe finish offers the same optimal scratch and chip resistance as our polished finish, protecting your peace of mind against anything life throws your way.

### 

Luxurious

Cambria Luxe captivates with an unparalleled shine that brings each design to life, enhancing every detail with a polished, lustrous depth.

### 

Guaranteed

All Cambria Luxe countertops come with an industry-leading, transferable [Full Lifetime Warranty](https://cambriausa.com/warranty).
