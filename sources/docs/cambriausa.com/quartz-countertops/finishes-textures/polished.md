## Benefits of Polished Finish

---

Cambria polished quartz countertops are known for their alluring look. But beyond the shining surface is the beautiful peace of mind knowing it was made to be carefree and durable for everyday use for life.

---

### 

Maintenance Free

Built with easy in mind, Cambria quartz countertops never need sealing, polishing, or reconditioning. Just clean with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap, if desired.

### 

Durable

As one of the hardest minerals on Earth, quartz is naturally strong and extremely resistant to chips and scratches. Its nonabsorbent nature means it’s also incredibly stain resistant.

### 

Luxurious

We craft our luxurious quartz designs by harnessing creative vision, industry-first innovation, and intricate processes to create the most unique and broadest design palette available.

### 

Guaranteed

All Cambria polished countertops come with an industry-leading, transferable [Full Lifetime Warranty](https://cambriausa.com/warranty).
