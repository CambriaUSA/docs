# Free & Fair Trade

**A Level Playing Field and Why It Matters to American Manufacturing** 

---

At Cambria, we believe that “American made” is more than just a label—it is a promise to do things the right way, in delivering ingenuity, craftsmanship, and reliability. It reflects a deep sense of pride in the work we do and the people we do it with. It represents a dedication to our employees, customers, and vendor partners. Further to that, our pursuit of free and fair trade is a sustained commitment to the American communities in which we work. All that’s Americana is a commitment to American capitalism, which is born of that same free and fair enterprise—and remains its greatest virtue.

We proudly engage with our U.S. domestic quartz surfacing industry to face the challenges of the day and righteously, justly, pursue the measures to ensure free and fair trade, and a level playing field for U.S. manufacturing workers and the companies that employ them.

Fabricated Imported Quartz

More fabricated imported quartz, pictured here, hits our shores every day. When this fabricated imported quartz is exported to the United States, it is the American stone fabricator who loses business. The countries responsible for this import surge threatens to shut down American competitors and take over 100% of the U.S. market across the entire quartz surfacing supply chain, including stone fabrication.

### About Cambria

Founded in 2000, Cambria is the leading family-owned, American-made producer of natural quartz surfaces.
