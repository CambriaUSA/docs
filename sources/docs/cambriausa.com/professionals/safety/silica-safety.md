## 

Silica Safety

Preventing respirable crystalline silica exposure is a top priority for our industry. We’re committed to providing information and resources to fabricators to support their work to protect their employees’ health and safety.

![cutting hand bridge tile](https://cambriausa.scene7.com/is/image/cambriacompanyllc/Cutting-Hand-Bridge-Tile-24-254?qlt=85&ts=1724941997613&dpr=off)

## Silica Safety Notice

---

We remain committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for our workers and believe that no worker in any segment of the industry should be exposed to dangerous levels of respirable crystalline silica in the workplace. View safe fabrication resources [here](https://cambriausa.com/professionals/safety/silica-safety.html#resources).

### 

OSHA Resources

Exposure to respirable crystalline silica within the stone industry is entirely preventable through strict compliance with OSHA standards. We encourage OSHA to continue to inform and work with the stone processing and fabrication sector to ensure compliance with its standards.
