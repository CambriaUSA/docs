## Stay Beautiful

---

Our quartz surfaces are durable, gorgeous, and easy to care for. Here’s how to maintain their elegance.

---

### 

Keep Them Clean

 
Clean regularly with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap, if desired.

### 

Keep Them Cool

 
Learn how to keep your Cambria in pristine condition by exploring our [Product Care information](https://cambriausa.com/quartz-countertops/product-care).

### 

Keep Away Harsh Chemicals

Abrasive and corrosive materials can harm your beautiful countertops. Care for them properly.
