## Transferable Full Lifetime Warranty

Every Cambria surface is backed by a transferable Full Lifetime Warranty — a powerful selling point for homebuyers that sets your builds apart. When a homeowner knows their countertops are guaranteed for life, it adds measurable value to your product and peace of mind to every sale. And if a repair is ever needed, Cambria's trained professionals handle it.

## Build America, Buy America Compliant

All Cambria quartz surface products comply with the Build America, Buy America Act (BABA) and Buy American Act — making Cambria the right specification for federally funded projects. If your work includes publicly funded construction, Cambria eliminates noncompliance risk and keeps your projects on track.
