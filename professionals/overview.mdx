## What's Available to Professionals

As a Cambria trade professional, you gain access to a comprehensive suite of tools and resources designed to elevate your business:

- **Complimentary samples** — request physical samples to share with clients and specify with confidence
- **Design resources** — browse the full quartz design catalog, inspiration galleries, and the latest Cambria Style issues
- **Technical specifications** — access product data sheets, spec PDFs, certifications, and installation guidance
- **Cambria University™** — immersive, hands-on training in fabrication, operations, installation, and safety for fabricators and installers across the U.S. and Canada; [learn more](/professionals/cambria-university)
- **Cambria Resource Hub** — a centralized hub with tools, sell sheets, and business resources; [access the Resource Hub](https://www.cambriausa.com/professionals/resources)
- **Safety resources** — silica safety guidelines, training videos, OSHA compliance support, and the SafetyFirst program; [explore safety resources](/professionals/safety)

## American Made. Free and Fair Trade.

Cambria's commitment to professionals goes beyond product — it reflects a deep belief in American manufacturing and free and fair trade. At Cambria, "American made" is more than a label — it is a promise to deliver ingenuity, craftsmanship, and reliability. It reflects pride in the work we do, the people we do it with, and the communities we serve.

All Cambria quartz surface products comply with the requirements in the [Build America, Buy America Act](/professionals/baba-compliance) and Buy American Act — making Cambria the right choice for federally funded infrastructure projects.
