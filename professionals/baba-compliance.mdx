## What Is the Build America, Buy America Act?

<Info>
  The **Build America, Buy America Act (BABA)** was enacted as Section 70901 of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act. It requires that all iron, steel, manufactured products, and construction materials used in federally funded infrastructure projects be produced in the United States. Projects that use non-compliant materials risk losing access to federal funding — a significant liability for builders, architects, and project owners.
</Info>

The BABA Act and the older Buy American Act together form the legal framework for domestic content requirements on federally funded projects. By specifying Cambria, you eliminate compliance risk from the start — no documentation headaches, no waiver requests, no delays.
