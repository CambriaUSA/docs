## Cambria University™

Cambria University™ offers fabricators and installers throughout the U.S. and Canada immersive, hands-on training designed to turn knowledge into expertise. Classes cover all areas of the business — from fabrication techniques and operations to installation methods and silica safety.

Whether you attend on-site in St. Peter, MN, or take advantage of virtual sessions and field visits to your own shop, Cambria University meets you where you are.

## Safe Fabrication

Cambria is deeply committed to promoting the health and safety of all workers in the stone fabrication and installation industry. The Cambria SafetyFirst program creates awareness about silica dust hazards and provides tools, resources, and training to help you maintain a safe and compliant workplace for your team.

Silica safety resources include training videos, OSHA compliance guidance, safety data sheets, and the Cambria University safety curriculum.
