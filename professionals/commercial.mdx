### Sustainability Certifications

Cambria's commitment to responsible manufacturing is validated by a full suite of sustainability and transparency certifications:

- **All Declare** — participates in the Declare Program for material transparency
- **Health Product Declaration (HPD)** — full HPD available for specification and LEED documentation
- **mindful MATERIALS** — proudly listed in the mindful MATERIALS library
- **Living Building Challenge compliant** — verified through the Declare program
- **SCS Global Services** — select Cambria products contain recycled content certified by SCS Global Services

## BABA Compliance for Federal Projects

<Info>
  All Cambria quartz surface products fully comply with the Build America, Buy America Act (BABA, Section 70901 of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act) and the Buy American Act. This eliminates the risk of noncompliance and ensures your projects remain eligible for federal funding — no waivers, no workarounds, no risk.
</Info>

Specifying Cambria on federally funded projects means you can move forward without compliance concerns. Cambria's U.S.-based manufacturing facility and domestic supply chain ensure that every product meets BABA requirements out of the box.
