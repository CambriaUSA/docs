Cambria University™ empowers fabricators and installers with immersive, hands-on training to deliver exceptional results on every project. Founded in 2005 in St. Peter, Minnesota, Cambria University has become a premier learning exchange center for the stone fabrication industry — a place where your experience meets Cambria's expertise to create safer, more productive, and more profitable fabrication operations.

## A Collaborative Learning Experience

Cambria University is fundamentally collaborative. Rather than delivering a one-size-fits-all curriculum, the University starts by understanding your priorities and challenges, then builds a custom learning track designed to drive your business forward. The mission is clear: promote high-quality, safe, innovative, and productive operations in the fabrication and installation of Cambria products.

> "We believe excellence is achieved through collaboration."

Available exclusively to Cambria business partners, training takes place both on-site at the University and through virtual sessions and field visits directly to your fabrication shop — meeting you wherever you work.

## Who It's For

Cambria University is available to **fabricators and installers throughout the United States and Canada** who are Cambria business partners. Whether you're a seasoned fabricator looking to refine your process or a newer shop building your team's skills, Cambria University has a learning track for you.

Training formats include:

- **On-site sessions** at the Cambria University facility in St. Peter, MN
- **Virtual learning** opportunities for remote access to coursework
- **Field visits** — Cambria University instructors come to your shop for on-location training

## Cambria University and the Bigger Picture

Cambria University training is not a standalone program — it's a core part of Cambria's commitment to excellence across the entire supply chain, from manufacturing to final installation. When fabricators and installers are well-trained, the result is better surfaces, safer workplaces, and more satisfied end customers.

The University also plays a central role in Cambria's **SafetyFirst** program, ensuring that silica safety best practices are embedded into every aspect of fabrication training.
