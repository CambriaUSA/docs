## Pillar 1: Family Owned, American Made

There is a humble confidence that comes from decades of hard work, commitment, and staying true to what matters most. Cambria is proudly family owned, American made, and deeply community focused — values that are woven into every surface produced.

### What "American Made" Means in Practice

- **Over 95% of costs** from every Cambria purchase are reinvested back into the U.S. economy — from raw material sourcing through final installation.
- **More than 30 distribution centers** across the US ensure that Cambria surfaces are always within reach for homeowners and trade professionals, with no overseas supply-chain delays.
- **Build America, Buy America (BABA) compliant** — all Cambria quartz surface products meet the requirements of the Build America, Buy America Act and the Buy American Act, making Cambria the preferred choice for federally funded construction projects.
- **Domestic supply chain reliability** — because manufacturing never leaves the country, you benefit from faster, more predictable delivery and a consistent quality standard on every single slab.

  Cambria recycles **more than one billion gallons of water annually** across its slab manufacturing and fabrication facilities, and uses renewable energy at its primary mine — a reflection of the responsibility Cambria feels toward American communities and the environment.
</Info>

Cambria's commitment to America goes beyond the factory floor. The CambriaCares initiative creates lasting impact by uplifting youth, championing education, and supporting strong, thriving communities across the country. Every Cambria purchase contributes to sustaining skilled tradespeople, designers, and manufacturers right here at home.

## Pillar 3: Performance Perfected

When it comes to quality, Cambria does not believe in shortcuts. Your countertops, vanity tops, and shower walls face heat, moisture, sharp edges, and spills every single day. Cambria surfaces are engineered to meet all of those challenges — and never show the strain.

### The Science Behind the Surface

| Performance Property | Specification |
|---|---|
| **Quartz Content** | 93% pure natural quartz |
| **Hardness (Mohs Scale)** | 7 out of 10 — harder than most natural stone |
| **Surface Type** | Nonporous and nonabsorbent |
| **Stain Resistance** | Resists wine, oil, coffee, acids, and common household chemicals |
| **Scratch Resistance** | Chip and scratch resistant for daily use |
| **Food Safety** | NSF International NSF-51 certified for food contact zones |
| **Maintenance** | Zero sealing, polishing, or reconditioning required |
| **VOC Emissions** | GREENGUARD Gold certified — low emissions for healthier indoor air |
