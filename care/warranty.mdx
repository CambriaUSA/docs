## What the Warranty Covers

Your Cambria Full Lifetime Warranty covers:

- **Manufacturing defects** — any defect arising from the manufacturing process
- **Surface integrity** — covered for the full lifetime of the original and all subsequent owners
- **Transferability** — when you sell your home, the warranty stays with the countertop and transfers automatically to the new owners

This means your Cambria surfaces are protected not just while you live in your home, but for as long as the countertop exists in any home.

## How to Register Your Cambria

Registering your Cambria product ensures that your warranty is on file and that Cambria can reach you with any important updates.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Registration Page">
    Go to [cambriausa.com/warranty](https://www.cambriausa.com/warranty) to access the product registration form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the Form">
    Fill in your contact information, installation details, and product information as prompted. All fields marked as required must be completed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Your Registration">
    Submit the completed form. You'll receive confirmation that your Cambria is registered and your Full Lifetime Warranty is active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If your Cambria product was purchased **prior to August 31, 2018**, the online registration form may not apply to your purchase. Please call Cambria Customer Care directly at **1-866-CAMBRIA** (1-866-226-2742) for warranty information specific to your product.
</Note>

## How to Submit a Warranty Claim

In the rare event that you experience an issue with your Cambria surface, submitting a claim is straightforward.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Call Cambria Customer Care">
    Contact Cambria directly by phone at **1-866-CAMBRIA** (1-866-226-2742). Customer Care professionals are available to take your call and open a claim on your behalf.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the Issue">
    A Customer Care representative will ask you about the nature of the issue, when it was noticed, and any relevant details about your installation. Have your product registration information handy if possible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Expert Technician Assessment">
    An expert field service technician will be dispatched to assess your surfaces in person. Cambria's technicians are trained to evaluate, document, and address surface concerns professionally.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resolution">
    Based on the technician's assessment, Cambria will take the appropriate steps to resolve your claim — whether that's finishing work, advanced surface cleaning, or a more comprehensive remedy covered under your warranty.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips to Keep Your Warranty Valid

Maintaining your surfaces according to Cambria's care guidelines ensures you get the full benefit of your warranty and keeps your countertops looking their best.

<Tip>
  **Keep them clean.** Wipe your surfaces regularly with warm water, a soft cotton cloth, and mild soap if desired. Avoid abrasive cleaners and scrub pads. See the full [Product Care guide](/care/product-care) for details.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Keep them cool.** Always use a closed-weave hot pad or solid trivet under pots, pans, slow cookers, air fryers, and any other heat-generating appliance or cookware. Direct or sustained heat can damage natural stone surfaces.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Avoid harsh chemicals.** Do not use bleach, oven cleaners, abrasive products, paint removers, or any strong alkaline or acidic cleaners. Stick to warm water and mild soap for everyday cleaning. See the full list in [What to Avoid](/care/what-to-avoid).
</Tip>
