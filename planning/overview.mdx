## What Every Cambria Project Involves

Regardless of which path you're on, every project follows a proven process that puts quality and your experience first.

### 1. Find Your Design

Start by browsing Cambria's industry-leading quartz design palette — the broadest collection of natural quartz surfaces available. Use the online design search filters to narrow by color, movement, and finish, or visit a Cambria Gallery or Showroom to see full slabs and installations in person.

### 2. Work With a Cambria Partner Professional

Cambria projects are completed through a network of vetted retail partners — fabricators, designers, and installers who know Cambria surfaces inside and out. Your professional partner guides you from consultation through completion, ensuring every detail is handled with expertise.

<Info>
  Ready to get connected? [Find a Cambria professional near you](/planning/finding-a-professional) to schedule a complimentary consultation and receive design resources, recommendations, and referrals.
</Info>

### 3. Measurement Appointment

Before any fabrication begins, a professional from your partner's team visits your space to take an official, precise measurement. This is when final design decisions — edge profiles, seams, cutouts — are confirmed. If you're replacing an existing countertop and purchasing a new sink or faucet, have those items on hand at this appointment.

### 4. Fabrication and Customization

Your Cambria slabs are reviewed and cut by trained fabrication partners. Edge profiles are shaped, cutouts are made, and every detail is refined to the exact specifications of your space.

### 5. Professional Installation

On your scheduled installation day, your Cambria surfaces are delivered and installed by professionals. Plan to be home for the appointment and have the space cleared and accessible. Your installer will walk you through final details before they leave.

### 6. Register Your Full Lifetime Warranty

Once installation is complete, register your Cambria surfaces to activate your Full Lifetime Warranty — the most comprehensive warranty in the natural quartz industry. Cambria's warranty is transferable, meaning it stays with the home even if you move.

<Note>
  Cambria's Regional & Field Service Support team provides complimentary post-installation services — including advanced surface cleaning, finishing work, and claim assistance — that go above and beyond the warranty itself.
</Note>
